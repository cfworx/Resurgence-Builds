## THE SYNERGY ENGINE

**12 engines running simultaneously:**

1. **In Sync (double-dip)** — Cast Scanning Pulse → instant skill hit → both weapon and skill buffs live → +18% Weapon Damage + +18% Skill Damage. Refreshed constantly by Smart Cover skill hits and SM fire. This is live from bullet one.
2. **Dominating Presence** — +20% Multi-Shot when 4+ enemies within 20m
3. **Phalanx Attack 4pc** — +12% Multi-Shot + +18% RoF + +18% Mag Size
4. **Inspiration (Backpack)** — +30% RoF to team while skill is deployed (Smart Cover is almost always deployed)
5. **Fury Strike 2pc** — Optimal Range extended beyond SM's already-solid 29m base
6. **Swift Taunt** — Hit 3 enemies in 5s → Extra Health = 25% Max HP for 10s (at 550 RPM, trivially always active)
7. **Glass Cannon** — +20% Weapon Damage, justified entirely by Swift Taunt's permanent Extra Health buffer
8. **Scanning Pulse** — +20% vulnerability multiplied across every Multi-Shot double-tap proc
9. **Smart Cover** — Skill hits feed In Sync's skill-side buff simultaneously with Pulse; team gets WD buff from Engineering conversion
10. **Tactical Link + Bugle Call** — +14.4% RoF spike during signature window, stacking on Phalanx and Inspiration bonuses
11. **Dominating Presence + Multi-Shot total** — Phalanx +12% + DP +20% + gear rolls = ~37–40% Multi-Shot. Every ~3rd SM bullet fires twice.
12. **In Sync + Glass Cannon + Dominating Presence stacked WD** — +18% (In Sync) + +20% (Glass Cannon) + +12% (DP base) = ~50% Weapon Damage boost running simultaneously before any other multipliers

## COMBAT ROTATION

**Pre-engage:**
Drop Smart Cover on cover your team will use. Cast Scanning Pulse — every enemy in range takes a skill hit. In Sync's skill-side buff activates instantly. Fire the SM — weapon hits activate In Sync's weapon-side buff. Both buffs are now live. **Both double to +18% Weapon Damage and +18% Skill Damage.** You are at full power from the first second.

**Sustained:**
Hold the trigger. At 550 RPM with Phalanx +18% RoF and Inspiration +30% RoF, you are hitting enemies fast enough that Swift Taunt (hit 3 enemies in 5s) is always active. Extra Health is always up. Glass Cannon's +20% WD is always justified. At 4+ enemies within 20m, Dominating Presence adds +20% Multi-Shot. Every ~3rd bullet fires twice into a 20% vulnerability debuff from Pulse.

**Signature window:**
Pop Tactical Link. Bugle Call adds +14.4% RoF on top of everything already running. This is the room-clear window.

**Reload:**
118-round magazine means fewer reloads. Take cover on reload. Re-engage immediately — In Sync refreshes within the first few shots of the new magazine.

**Re-cycle:**
Pulse comes back up → recast → In Sync skill-side refreshed. Smart Cover refreshes → recast. Both skills feeding In Sync continuously for the duration of the fight.
