Division Resurgence is running a Double XP weekend right now. Both regular XP and Specialization XP are doubled, the event is already live, and it ends Sunday at midnight. If you have been sitting on a half-leveled Specialization or a build you have been meaning to push, this is your window.

## The Official Announcement

The Division Resurgence team posted the following on X on June 12, 2026 at 9:00 AM:

> "This weekend, regular and Specialization XP is doubled! Starting NOW and lasting until Sunday at midnight, we're having fun with the game and letting you grab more experience all around. Where are you going to grind first?!"

The [announcement post](https://x.com/TheDivMobile/status/2065464140706038216) includes a promotional image showing agents walking through a New York street with a burning storefront in the background.

The in-game notification (screenshot above) confirms the details directly: "From now until Sunday at midnight, all regular and Specialization Experience gains are doubled across all activities. Prepare your loadout and take full advantage of the increased experience — every activity counts!"

## What Is Confirmed

Quoting only what the official post and in-game notification actually state:

- **Both regular XP and Specialization XP are doubled.** Not just one or the other. Both are running at 2x.
- **All activities are included.** The in-game notification specifically says "across all activities."
- **The event started on June 12, 2026** (the post went live at 9:00 AM that morning).
- **The event runs until Sunday at midnight.** That puts the end of the window at the conclusion of Sunday, June 14, 2026.
- **No additional cost.** This is a server-side weekend event applied to all players.

What the post does NOT specify:
- The exact timezone for "midnight" (most live service events run on UTC, but the post does not state which)
- Whether the multiplier stacks with any active XP boost consumables or Classified Ops Pass bonuses

We are not going to guess on either of those. If the team posts a clarification, we will update this article.

## What "Double Specialization XP" Actually Means

This is the part that gets buried when people only read "Double XP" and move on. Specialization XP is the slower track. Hitting Specialization milestones unlocks the Specialization-tier passives and abilities that round out your build identity. Demolitionist, Tech Operator, Bulwark, Vanguard, Field Medic. Each one has its own Specialization track with its own XP requirements.

When the team runs a regular Double XP weekend, that usually only juices your character level. This one juices both, which means it is uniquely good for:

- Players still climbing the main level cap
- Players who maxed level but have a Specialization sitting at 60-70% completion
- Players who recently switched into a new Specialization and are starting that track fresh

If you have ever grinded out Specialization XP at the regular rate, you know why this matters. The XP curve for higher Specialization tiers is not friendly. A 2x weekend on top of that curve is a significant chunk off your grind.

## Where to Grind: Smart Plan for the Weekend

The official post asked "Where are you going to grind first?!" so we may as well answer it. Here is a practical priority list for getting the most out of the 2x window.

### 1. Weekly Bounties Before Reset

Weekly bounties give some of the chunkiest XP rewards in the game per hour invested. Knock those out first while the multiplier is live.

### 2. Hostile Nest Runs

Hostile Nests reward solid XP per clear and let you stack up loot at the same time. If you have a group, this is where the multiplier pays off the fastest, because clear times drop hard with a coordinated squad.

### 3. Conflicts (PvP) for the PvP Crowd

If you are one of those unhinged individuals who voluntarily queue into Conflicts on a Friday night instead of watching Netflix like a normal human being, congratulations: the weekend is your Super Bowl. XP from matches counts toward both tracks. Be mindful that match length varies, so XP per hour can swing.

### 4. Side Missions You Have Been Putting Off

If you have stalled side missions in your log, this is the weekend to clean them up. Side mission XP is reliable and predictable. Worth noting: there are active bugs on the [public Trello known issues board](/news/division-resurgence-known-issues-trello-board-breakdown/) for certain side missions, including the Outpost Annihilation issue. If a mission stalls, do not bash your head on it. Pick a different one.

### 5. Specialization-Tied Activities

If a specific Specialization track gives bonus XP for certain activities (skill kills, headshots, melee finishers, etc.), make sure you are leaning into those during the weekend. The Specialization XP multiplier compounds with how you play.

## Honest Reality Check

A few things to keep in mind so you do not torch your weekend chasing the wrong activity.

**Server population matters.** Weekend events tend to bring more players in. Matchmaking should be faster than normal, which is a tailwind for any group activity.

**Burnout is real.** Eight straight hours of Hostile Nests on a Saturday is how you learn to hate a game you love. Pace yourself. The multiplier is on for roughly 63 hours from announcement to Sunday midnight. You do not need to be online for all of them. Your kids and your spouse still remember what you look like. Probably.

**Active bug awareness.** Before you commit a long session to a specific activity, glance at the public Trello known issues board linked above in this article. If items disappearing from inventory or specific mission progression bugs are live, you want to know before you waste 2x time on a broken loop.

## When Does the Double XP Weekend End?

The official post says "Sunday at midnight." The in-game notification confirms the same. Treating that at face value means the event ends at the conclusion of Sunday, June 14, 2026. If you are running this article on Saturday or early Sunday, you still have time. If you are reading this on Monday, the event is over but we will update this post if any extension is announced.
