## The Numbers Are In

The Division Resurgence crossed 1 million worldwide downloads in its launch week, with approximately 380,000 of those coming from the United States alone. First-week revenue reached approximately $303,000.

### Week 1 vs Week 2

| Metric | Week 1 | Week 2 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Downloads (Worldwide) | ~1,000,000 | ~230,000 | −77% |
| Downloads (US) | ~380,000 | ~70,000 | −81% |
| Revenue | ~$303,000 | ~$284,000 | **−6.1%** |

The download drop looks dramatic at first glance, but analysts describe this as a textbook "Post-Launch Acquisition Normalization Curve" rather than a failure signal. The important number is revenue retention — despite losing 77% of new downloads, revenue only dipped 6.1%. This means the core monetizing audience found the game and stuck with it.

### Community Reception

Professional reviews have been cautiously positive:

**TheSixthAxis** highlighted the console-quality gameplay feel, intuitive controls, and inclusion of Dark Zone and PvP at launch. They noted the story doesn't fully deliver and progression feels gated.

**Pocket Gamer** praised the thorough translation of the Division formula to mobile, calling the shooting "impactful" and the content variety "extensive." Graphics were noted as device-dependent.

### Community Feedback from Reddit

From early discussions on r/thedivisionresurgence:

- Core Division gameplay loop is intact and feels solid on mobile
- Controller support works but needs tuning refinements
- Resource constraints start hitting around level 30
- Significant network and login issues at launch, especially in APAC regions
- Touch controls occasionally register phantom inputs

### What's Next

The development team is actively patching — version 1.1.1 is the current build as of mid-April, addressing connectivity issues, audio bugs, and PvP scoreboard display errors. The team has signaled interest in PvP balancing updates to address dominant one-shot sniper builds.

We'll continue tracking the game's post-launch trajectory and will cover any major content updates as they're announced.
