## Agents, Welcome to Manhattan

The Division Resurgence officially launched worldwide on March 31, 2026 on iOS and Android as a free-to-play third-person shooter RPG. Developed by Ubisoft Mobile Games in Paris, the game brings a full Division experience to mobile devices with a canon storyline, open-world Manhattan, Dark Zone PvPvE, and co-op gameplay.

### Launch Times

| Region | Launch Time |
|--------|------------|
| Asia-Pacific | 7:00 AM UTC (9:00 AM CEST) |
| Americas, Europe | 11:00 AM UTC (1:00 PM CEST / 4:00 AM PDT) |

### What's Available at Launch

The game ships with a massive amount of content for a mobile title:

**PvE Content:**
- Full main story campaign set in Manhattan during the Green Poison outbreak
- 100+ dynamic world activities, patrols, and faction nests
- Lone Wolf Challenges — solo dungeons with tiered difficulty (Tier 1-5 to Tier 11+)
- Full co-op support for all missions with matchmaking
- Clan missions with facility upgrades

**PvP Content:**
- Dark Zone PvPvE with contaminated loot, extraction helicopters, and rogue agent mechanics
- Conflict modes: Skirmish and Domination (with power normalization)

### Five Specializations

| Specialization | Role | Best For |
|---------------|------|----------|
| Demolitionist | Burst DPS / Crowd Control | Solo play, clearing |
| Bulwark | Tank / Frontline | Aggression, solo |
| Vanguard | Recon / Tactical | PvP, team intel |
| Field Medic | Healer / Support | Group survival |
| Tech Operator | Drone Specialist | Sustained DPS (unlocked via progression) |

Each specialization has a full skill tree requiring 49 points to max out.

### Platform Support

The game runs on iOS and Android with full cross-play and cross-progression via Ubisoft Connect. Controller support includes PS5 DualSense, Xbox, and Backbone controllers. PC play is available through Ubisoft Connect with 4K resolution and unlocked FPS.

**Minimum Requirements:** Android 9.0+ or iOS 12.0+ with 4 GB RAM. Install size is approximately 10.5 GB (17 GB recommended free space).

### Launch Week Rewards

All players who log in during launch week receive the FIREFIRE gear set, Teddy Bear Patch, and can earn a Phoenix Weapon Skin through Twitch Drops (90 minutes of watching enabled streams).

See you in Manhattan, Agent.
