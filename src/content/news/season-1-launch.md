## Season 1 Has Arrived

After months of anticipation, **Season 1 of The Division Resurgence** officially launched today across all platforms. This is the largest content update since launch, introducing a new specialization, three gear sets, expanded endgame, and sweeping weapon balance changes.

Here's everything you need to know.

## New Specialization: Vanguard

The **Vanguard** is the game's first true frontline specialization. Built around aggression and close-quarters dominance, Vanguard agents excel at drawing fire and controlling space.

**Key features:**
- Signature skill: Bulwark Shield with enhanced melee capabilities
- Passive tree focused on armor regeneration and threat generation
- Unique OS Protocol synergies with Toughness-spec protocols
- Two dedicated skill mod combo sets: Spearhead and Blitz Tactics

Check out our [Vanguard Frontline Tank build](/builds/vanguard-frontline-tank/) for a day-one endgame setup.

## Three New Gear Sets

| Gear Set | Archetype | 4-Piece Bonus |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Heartbreaker | DPS (Body Shots) | Stacking bonus armor and weapon damage on body shots |
| Umbra Initiative | Team DPS | Creates weakness zones behind enemies for amplified team damage |
| Rigger | Skill DPS | Destroying skills returns them with full charges and bonus damage |

Full bonus breakdowns available in our [Gear Sets database](/database/gear-sets/).

## Two New Strongholds

- **Capitol Building** — 4-player endgame mission with three boss encounters
- **Roosevelt Island** — Open-area stronghold with dynamic spawns and a helicopter boss fight

Both available on Normal, Hard, Challenging, and Legendary difficulties.

## Weapon Balance Pass

Major changes to LMGs and Marksman Rifles. See our full breakdown in the [Patch 1.2.0 notes](/patch-notes/patch-1-2-0/).

**Winners:** RPK-74 (+8% base damage), M700 Carbon (+5% headshot multiplier)
**Losers:** AUG (-3% base damage, still needs work), SCAR-H (no changes, still D tier)
