## How to Claim Your Cross-Game Rewards

The cross-game rewards program between The Division 2 and The Division Resurgence is now live. Players who link both games to the same Ubisoft Connect account can unlock exclusive items in both titles.

### How to Link Your Accounts

1. Open The Division Resurgence
2. Go to Settings → Ubisoft Connect
3. Log in with your Ubisoft Connect account (create one if you don't have one)
4. Do the same in The Division 2
5. Both games must be linked to the **same** Ubisoft Connect account
6. Go to the Ubisoft Connect Rewards section in either game to manually claim rewards

**Rewards do not appear automatically** — you must manually claim them through the Ubisoft Connect Rewards menu.

### Rewards for Resurgence Players (Unlocked in Division 2)

| Reward | Type |
|--------|------|
| Resurgence Hat | Cosmetic |
| Resurgence Hoodie | Cosmetic |
| Resurgence Shirt | Cosmetic |
| Level 40 Boost | Progression (skip to Warlords of New York endgame) |

The Level 40 Boost is a significant unlock — it lets you skip directly to the Warlords of New York endgame content in Division 2, which is where the current endgame loop lives.

### Anniversary Caches

As part of the 10th Anniversary celebration, Anniversary Caches are available in Resurgence. Each cache guarantees a high-end item with a 5% chance for an exotic component. These are available through gameplay activities during the anniversary event period.
