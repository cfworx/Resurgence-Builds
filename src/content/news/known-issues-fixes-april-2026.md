## Current Version: 1.1.1

The Division Resurgence is actively being patched post-launch. Here are all known issues as of mid-April 2026, with workarounds where available.

## Connectivity & Login Issues

### Error 602 / Network Handshake Failures

**Problem:** Players get kicked to login screen with Error 602 or similar network failures. Most common in Asia-Pacific regions.

**Workarounds:**
- Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data
- Change DNS to `1.1.1.1` (Cloudflare) or `8.8.8.8` (Google)
- Use a VPN — Germany or Netherlands servers work best
- Clear the game's cache from your device settings

### Wi-Fi/Data Switching Disconnect

**Problem:** Game disconnects when your phone auto-switches from 5G to Wi-Fi mid-session. The IP change is interpreted as a security risk by the server.

**Workaround:** Lock your device to one connection type before launching the game. Turn off auto-switch in your device's network settings.

## Gameplay Bugs

### Persistent Notification Icon

**Problem:** Exclamation point notification icons won't clear even after viewing all items.

**Status:** Cosmetic bug only. No workaround. Expected fix in a future patch.

### Crafting Station Appears Frozen

**Problem:** The crafting progress bar looks frozen and doesn't move.

**Explanation:** This is not a bug. Crafting uses a **24-hour real-time timer**. The progress bar moves in real time, so it appears frozen during normal play sessions. Check back after 24 hours.

### Dark Zone Loot Loss on Crash

**Problem:** If the game crashes or force-closes during a Dark Zone extraction, all contaminated loot is lost permanently.

**Workaround:** Extract frequently. Don't hoard contaminated items. If your device tends to crash, extract after every 2-3 contaminated pickups.

### Open World Reward Cap

**Problem:** After completing 10 open-world activities in a single daily reset, rewards stop dropping.

**Explanation:** This is intentional, not a bug. The daily cap is 10 rewarded activities. Prioritize high-value activities (faction nests, dynamic events) first each day.

### Extraction Timer Desync

**Problem:** The extraction countdown timer stutters by 1-2 seconds compared to server time, sometimes causing premature zone exits or missed extractions.

**Workaround:** Leave 2-3 extra seconds of buffer when timing your extractions. Don't cut it close.

### PvP Scoreboard Bug

**Problem:** Incorrect kill counts displayed in post-match PvP scoreboards.

**Status:** Visual-only bug. Noted in April 13, 2026 patch notes as a priority fix.

### Social/Friends Search Bug

**Problem:** Cannot find friends or clan members via the search menu.

**Status:** Known issue. No workaround besides adding friends through Ubisoft Connect directly.

### Android Audio Bug

**Problem:** No sound on specific Android chipsets.

**Status:** Hotfix released in the April 13, 2026 patch. Update to the latest version. If the issue persists, try clearing the game cache and restarting.

## Performance Tips

If the game is running slowly:

1. Enable **Battery Saver** mode in game settings (reduces visual quality but improves FPS)
2. Close all background apps before launching
3. Play plugged in — thermal throttling on battery can drop FPS
4. **Recommended specs:** Android 13+ or iOS 16+ with 8 GB+ RAM for best experience
5. Minimum specs: Android 9.0+ or iOS 12.0+ with 4 GB RAM (expect lower settings)
