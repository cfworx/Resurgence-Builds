A clear weekly cadence helps you avoid bouncing between activities at random once you hit the Level 40 endgame.

## Daily Routine
- **DZ vendor check:** The vendor rotates stock every 24 hours. Always check for Chipsets or Augmentations.
- **Open-world activities:** Complete these to reveal OS Bounty Lieutenants.
- **DZ Runs:** 1–2 chipset DZ runs with targeted loot set to your specialization.
- **Daily commendation pickups:** Claim free SHD Keys here; there's no need to spend real money on caches.

## Weekly Routine
- **Complete the OS Bounty main target:** Make sure to **claim from the Bounty Menu**, not just the boss body, to get your OS Protocol.
- **Legendary Elite Missions:** Run your 2 bonus Legendary Elite Mission clears for the Tier 2 bonus caches.
- **Lone Wolf Challenge:** This challenge refreshes every 3 weeks (fixed in the Phase 2 update).

## Avoid the FOMO Traps
> [!WARNING]
> - **Sealed Caches** are a money sink — pull only with earned SHD Keys.
> - **Don't dismantle** anything Tier 2 until you're absolutely sure it's truly inferior to what you already have equipped.
