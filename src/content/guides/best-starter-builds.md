## You Gotta Start Somewhere

You just finished the campaign, hit the endgame, and realized your mismatched purple gear isn't cutting it anymore. You don't have the Lady Death. You don't have perfect T2 stat rolls. You're broke.

Don't panic. You don't need a mathematically perfect setup to start farming. You just need a cohesive plan. Here are the best starter builds that function perfectly fine with basic High-End (Gold) gear and zero exotics.

### 1. Pure Environmental Terrorism (Jackpot Chemical Medic)
A ridiculously easy-to-farm skill damage build. You just need the Jackpot 4-piece set and some basic Engineering stats. Throw chemical traps, deploy the hive, and watch the room melt. 
👉 **[Read the Jackpot Chemical Medic Build Guide](/builds/skill-meta-jackpot-chemical-8/)**

### 2. Babysitting Idiots 101 (Legendary Healer)
If your gear sucks, the easiest way to get carried through hard content is to play a dedicated healer. The Captain Cow set is easy to farm, and dropping a massive healing station guarantees you a spot in any group.
👉 **[Read the Legendary Healer Build Guide](/builds/legendary-healer-field-medic-5/)**

### 3. The Geneva Suggestion (AOE Chem Medic)
Another excellent variant of the Medic that focuses entirely on area denial. It's incredibly forgiving because you don't actually need to shoot your guns—just throw acid at spawn doors.
👉 **[Read the Geneva Suggestion Build Guide](/builds/geneva-suggestion-field-medic-12/)**
