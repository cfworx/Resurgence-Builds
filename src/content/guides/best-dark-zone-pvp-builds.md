## Welcome to the Toxicity

The Dark Zone is not a place for fair fights or moral high grounds. It is a place where you bring mathematically broken builds to steal other people's hard-earned loot. If you're tired of getting ganked at extraction by a 4-stack of sweaty rogues, it's time to adapt.

Here are the undisputed best Dark Zone PvP builds in The Division Resurgence.

### 1. The Hate Mail Generator (Lady Death Vanguard)
The #1 meta PvP build in the game right now. Insane movement speed, wallhacks on demand, and SMG burst damage that drops agents before they can even pop a medkit.

### 2. The 24-0 Spawn Trapper (Warlord Vanguard)
Designed specifically for Conflict and aggressive DZ pushes. The Warlord assault rifle combined with the Vanguard's tactical shield makes you virtually unkillable in 1v1 trades.
👉 **[Read the Fearless Warlord PvP Build Guide](/builds/conflict-pvp-fearless-warlord/)**

### 3. The All-Around Oxy Nuke (Field Medic)
Don't let the "Medic" name fool you. ProjectPandaTv's Oxidizing Swarm build stacks 1,700 Engineering and Glass Cannon to melt entire squads of rogues in seconds. Just stand in the back.
👉 **[Read the Oxidizing Swarm Medic Build Guide](/builds/panda-oxidizing-swarm-medic-14/)**

### 4. The Cross-Map Coward (Sharpshooter)
For the discerning agent who prefers to extract loot from 100 meters away. If you have the aim to land headshots, this build guarantees a one-shot kill on almost anyone out of cover.
