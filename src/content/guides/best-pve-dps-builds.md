## Stop Dragging Down Your Team

If you're queueing into Legendary missions with a haphazard collection of gear you picked up off the floor and wondering why you're getting instantly vaporized, this guide is for you. Below is the definitive list of the best PvE DPS builds in The Division Resurgence. 

Pick a lane, farm the gear, and stop making the Field Medic revive you every 30 seconds. Pair these with the [Center-ADS Release](/guides/center-ads-release-cover-peek-guide/) cover-peek technique — most of these builds assume you can do it.

### 1. The Alt-F4 Shotgun Button (Vanguard)
The absolute king of burst DPS. This Vanguard build uses the Double Barrel Shotgun and Explosive Rounds to instantly delete elites before they even finish their spawn animations. 
👉 **[Read the Vanguard Shotgun Build Guide](/builds/pve-meta-vanguard-shotgun-7/)**

### 2. Michael Bay's Wet Dream (Demolitionist)
If you prefer turning the entire screen into a low-framerate explosion simulator, the Demolitionist Artillery Turret build is unmatched for AoE clearing.
👉 **[Read the Demolitionist Explosive Chaos Build Guide](/builds/demolitionist-explosive-chaos-4/)**

### 3. The Flashbang Salesman (Tech Operator)
Why shoot enemies when you can blind them, bleed them, and let your Striker Drone do the heavy lifting? The ultimate crowd-control DPS hybrid.
👉 **[Read the Tech Operator Lockdown Build Guide](/builds/lockdown-tech-op-6/)**

### 4. The Unkillable Mosquito (Bulwark Tank)
Technically a tank build, but the Strawberry Milkshake exotic LMG turns you into a lifestealing DPS monster that simply refuses to die, no matter how many bad decisions you make.
👉 **[Read the Bulwark Tank Lifesteal Build Guide](/builds/vampire-bulwark-tank-9/)**
