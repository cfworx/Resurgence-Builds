# The Lockdown Tech Op — Disorient-Loop Striker Drone

## What is a fun off-meta Tech Operator build in The Division: Resurgence?

**A fun off-meta Tech Operator build pairs two 3-piece skill mod sets at the same time — EMCAT to make every drone crit apply Disoriented, and Stabilized Flight to feed +6% Skill Critical Hit Chance back to you on every Drone Lock. The result is a self-feeding crowd-control loop: your drone disorients an enemy → you crit harder because of Stabilized Flight → the crit applies Disoriented to the next enemy → repeat. It is the opposite of the Firewall-3pc meta everyone else runs, and every line of it is verbatim from the build database.**

A **Tech Operator** build for The Division: Resurgence built around **chained Disorient and skill-crit feedback**, not raw drone damage. Off-meta, mechanic-accurate, math on the page.

## The Thesis — How The Loop Works

Most Tech Op guides aim the build at one thing: maximum Striker Drone DPS. This build aims at something more interesting — **a closed feedback loop** where every drone crit pays for the next one in two ways simultaneously.

The loop has three beats:

1. **Striker Drone crits an enemy.** Because you run **EMCAT 3pc**, that crit applies **Disoriented** verbatim from the spreadsheet ("Striker Drone Critical Hits apply Disoriented to target enemies").
2. **Applying Drone Lock fires Stabilized Flight 3pc:** "Applying Drone Lock grants +6% Skill Critical Hit Chance for 20 seconds." That bonus stays up on every drone re-targeting.
3. **More skill crit = more EMCAT triggers** = more Disoriented uptime across the room = enemies miss more shots and walk into your AoE. Run the loop for the full 84-second Skill Duration Support window and the entire encounter is CC'd while your drone clips them down.

The drone is not your damage. The drone is your **status-effect printer**, and Concerted Strike + Brainstorm + Warlord's ceasefire bonus turns each loop into your real damage spike.

> Build philosophy: this build does NOT pretend Disoriented is a team-wide debuff that gives allies damage bonuses. The spreadsheet text on EMCAT says "Disoriented to target enemies" — that is the verified read. Disoriented affects the **enemy** (reduced accuracy, in-game text "less likely to hit"), it is not a team buff. We do not stack a damage-on-debuffed-enemy fantasy on top of it. The build's value is **fewer incoming bullets** and **more outgoing crits**, both verifiable in the data.

## Why Not Firewall? The Off-Meta Pitch

Almost every Tech Op build on the internet runs a Firewall set or a max-damage Striker setup. This build avoids both because:

- **You will be running the meta build in every Conflict lobby** if you pick Firewall. Goal here is fun and different — something nobody else in your matchmaking session is running.
- **EMCAT is a Specialized rarity skill mod** — easier to find than the High-End sets and just as build-defining when you actually pair it with the right partner mod.
- **The disorient loop creates a "soft tank" effect** without spending any toughness budget. You are tankier in real fights even though your sheet toughness is unchanged, because the enemies you are looking at miss more.

This is exactly the kind of "fun, viable, not what everyone else is running" build the off-meta brief is asking for.

<a id="the-drone"></a>

## The Drone — Striker, Not Disruptor

You run **Striker Drone** as your primary skill. Striker is the only drone that applies Drone Lock and the only drone EMCAT and Stabilized Flight target verbatim ("Striker Drone Critical Hits..." / "Applying Drone Lock grants..."). The Disruptor Drone variants do not benefit from these mods.

Second skill is a **Striker Hive**. The Hive is not the star — it exists as the **third skill mod proc body** so you can carry three skill mod pieces on the Hive slot. Hive deploys also count as a "Skill Deploy" for any deploy-triggered talent (Energize, Open Fire, Prime Engineer if rolled), and the Hive's micro-drones still apply hits within 20m which feeds Concerted Strike stacks.

If you want a non-drone second skill, **Smart Cover** is a legitimate swap — it gives you the same skill-deploy trigger and adds Skill Damage from Overwatch backpack if you ever rebuild around cover play. The mod loadout in this guide assumes Striker Hive.

## Per-Piece Stat Sheet — Every Slot, Every Stat

Every gear piece rolls **one Core Attribute** (Firepower / Toughness / Engineering) plus **two random Bonus Attributes** from the matching pool. For this build every Core is **Engineering** because the drone scales off Engineering and every Skill Crit / Skill Duration / Skill Cooldown stat is in the Engineering bonus pool.

| Slot | Core | Bonus #1 | Bonus #2 | Brand / Talent |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mask | Engineering | Skill Critical Chance | Skill Critical Damage | Jackpot OR Long-term Effect |
| Chest | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Duration | **T2 chest with Deep Focus** |
| Backpack | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Duration | **T2 bag with Brainstorm** |
| Gloves | Engineering | Skill Critical Chance | Skill Critical Damage | Jackpot OR Long-term Effect |
| Holster | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Critical Chance | Jackpot OR Long-term Effect |
| Knee Pads | Engineering | Skill Critical Damage | Skill Cooldown Recovery | Jackpot OR Long-term Effect |

### Why those exact stats

- **Skill Critical Chance (3 pieces)** — every point feeds the EMCAT loop. Higher SCC = more drone crits = more Disoriented procs = more enemies missing you. Plus Stabilized Flight feeds back another +6% SCC on every Drone Lock, so your sheet number compounds in real fights.
- **Skill Critical Damage (3 pieces)** — Jackpot 4pc already grants +24%. Per-piece rolls push toward double-damage drone crits on every other shot.
- **Skill Intensity (chest, backpack, holster)** — multiplies the base damage of every drone shot. The crit and multi-shot multipliers ride on top of a bigger base when SI is stacked.
- **Skill Cooldown Recovery (knees)** — drone has a real cooldown if it gets shot down. SCR shortens the gap.

### Stats to NOT chase (reroll on sight)

- **Skill Multi-Shot Chance** — useful but lower priority than Crit Chance + Crit Damage on this build because EMCAT triggers from **crits**, not multi-shots. Multi-shot rolls are fine secondary picks; never replace a Crit Chance roll with a Multi-Shot roll.
- **Healing Intensity** — wrong build entirely.
- **All Firepower bonus stats** — wrong Core.

## The Math — One Disorient Loop, End-to-End

Scenario: Striker Drone deployed at a 5-enemy elite pack. Base Striker Drone shot = **2,400 raw damage** before any multipliers (engineering-scaled estimate at endgame Core values).

### Skill-Crit stack (your effective Skill Crit Damage per shot)

| Multiplier | Source | Effective SCD |
|---|---|---|
| Base (no extra SCD) | — | +0% |
| Jackpot 4pc | +24% Skill Crit Damage | +24% |
| Concerted Strike OS main stat | +0% (this OS slot rolls Weapon Damage as main, not SCD) | — |
| Per-piece SCD (3 pieces × ~9% each) | Engineering Core bonus rolls | ~+27% |
| Drone Overload signature (active) | +100% Skill Crit Damage for 12s | +100% |
| **Total SCD when Drone Overload is up** | | **~+151%** |

### Skill-Crit-Chance stack (probability a given drone shot crits)

| Multiplier | Source | SCC |
|---|---|---|
| Jackpot 3pc | +10.8% | +10.8% |
| Stabilized Flight 3pc | +6% on every Drone Lock | +6% |
| Per-piece SCC (3 pieces × ~9% each) | Engineering bonus rolls | ~+27% |
| **Total SCC on this build** | (additive, before any base SCC from leveling) | **~+43.8%** |

That means **roughly 4 of every 10 drone shots crit**, every crit applies Disoriented to the target, and during Drone Overload windows those crits hit for roughly **2.5× normal damage**.

### Concerted Strike OS damage stack (your weapon damage, while drone is hitting)

| Multiplier | Source | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Concerted Strike main stat | +15% Weapon Damage | × 1.15 |
| Concerted Strike talent (3 stacks) | +24% Damage when skill hits within 20m | × 1.24 |
| Brainstorm-driven Skill Duration | Longer drone uptime = more talent uptime | (uptime multiplier) |
| **Your weapon damage while drone is engaged** | | **× 1.43 baseline** |

### One full loop in numbers

Drone fires a burst (4 shots), ~40% crit rate → 1"“2 crits → 1"“2 Disoriented applications per burst → enemy loses meaningful accuracy → your AR + Concerted Strike chunks them with ×1.43 weapon damage AND your sheet SCC just gained another +6% from the next Drone Lock trigger. Every 1 second of drone fire, every enemy in the pack is either Disoriented (if hit by a crit) or about to be.

> The math says: an elite group of 5 enemies will have **at least 2-3 of them Disoriented at any given second** once the drone is fully ramped. That is functionally a permanent debuff field with zero downtime on your part.

## Skill Mod Choices — EMCAT 3pc + Stabilized Flight 3pc

You run **two different 3-piece skill mod sets simultaneously**: one on the Striker Drone, one on the Striker Hive. The drone gets EMCAT for the Disoriented application. The Hive gets Stabilized Flight so the +6% SCC trigger fires every time the Hive's micro-drones tag an enemy (the spreadsheet text triggers on "Applying Drone Lock").

| Skill Slot | Mod Set | 2pc | 3pc | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Striker Drone** | **EMCAT** | Skill Health +4"“8% | Striker Drone Critical Hits apply Disoriented to target enemies | This is the Disoriented printer. |
| **Striker Hive** | **Stabilized Flight** | Skill Health +4"“8% | Applying Drone Lock grants +6% Skill Critical Hit Chance for 20 seconds | Feeds the crit loop back to your sheet SCC. |

### Why not Lethal Sweep / Optimized Targeting / etc.

- **Lethal Sweep 3pc** ("Striker Drone Multi-Shot hits grant bonus Skill Critical Hit Chance stacks") is the obvious meta pick. It is great. But it requires you to land **Multi-Shots**, and you are not stacking Multi-Shot Chance — you are stacking Crit Chance. Stabilized Flight is the better partner here because Drone Lock fires every time the drone re-targets, which happens far more often than a Multi-Shot proc.
- **Optimized Targeting** is fine on a pure DPS build. It does nothing for the Disoriented loop and it does not feed your weapon damage.
- **Supercharged 2pc** is a tempting partial because it grants self-heal on drone deploy — but you only get the 2pc out of it before the 3pc kicks in, and you would lose the Stabilized Flight 3pc. The build wants the feedback loop more than the heal.

<a id="why-concerted-strike"></a>

## Why Concerted Strike Earns the OS Slot

Concerted Strike (Firepower, High-End) reads: *"Skill Hits within 20 meters have a 100% chance of granting +8% Damage for 10 seconds, up to 3 stacks. 1 seconds cooldown."*

Three reasons it wins here over Side by Side, Mechanized Support, or Augmented Lethality:

1. **100% chance trigger.** No proc chain to roll for — every time your drone or Hive lands a hit inside 20m, you get a stack. Three stacks = +24% Damage, refreshed continuously.
2. **+8% Damage applies to skill AND weapon hits** (the spreadsheet text reads "Damage", not "Weapon Damage"). So your drone's own damage benefits from the same OS that the drone is feeding.
3. **+15% Weapon Damage main stat** stacks on top — every weapon shot from your AR gets the OS main stat AND the talent bonus simultaneously.

Alternates if you cannot land Concerted Strike:
- **Side by Side** (Superior) — +6% Firepower while within 30m of a deployed Skill. Solid bridge.
- **Mechanized Support** (Superior) — +15% Weapon Damage while within 20m of a deployed Skill. Simpler, flat bonus, easier to drop.

<a id="why-deep-focus"></a>

## Why Deep Focus + Brainstorm Owns the Skill Slots

Deep Focus (chest) reads: *"+28% Skill Duration but inflicts -10% Skill Radius."*

Brainstorm (backpack) reads: *"+15% Skill Duration. +15% more when within 20m of an ally."*

- Drone base duration is short by default; Skill Duration extends time-on-field where the entire build's value lives.
- The −10% Radius from Deep Focus does not hurt this build — your drone targets individual enemies, not an AoE.
- Brainstorm's ally-proximity bonus stacks cleanly with team play. Solo, you still get +15% baseline.

Combined: roughly **+43"“58% Skill Duration** from the two pieces alone, before any per-piece Skill Duration bonus rolls.

## The Weapon — Warlord (or the Non-Exotic Backup)

### Primary: Warlord (Exotic AR, T1)

Warlord's **Play Rough** talent reads verbatim: *"While firing, reduces Incoming Damage by 25%. When firing ceases, applies 60% of the reduced damage and grants +10% Skill Damage for 10 seconds."*

Why it fits this build:
- **25% DR while firing** — you are an Engineering-Core build with weak personal defense. This is a real survival layer.
- **+10% Skill Damage on ceasefire** — every time you pause your AR fire (to reposition, to reload, to swap to drone-watching), your drone gains a 10s damage window. The build forces you to ceasefire constantly anyway.
- **AR fits at the range your drone wants to engage at** — Striker Drone has a ~25m effective bubble; Warlord's 29m optimal range matches.

### Non-exotic backup: T1 AR with Adaptation + Killer

If you do not have Warlord, chase **Police M4 (T1 AR)** or **Enhanced ACR-E (T1)** rolling:

| Talent | Effect | Why it fits |
|---|---|---|
| **Adaptation** | Body shots → +4.8% Skill Duration for 8s. Headshots → +6% Skill Intensity for 8s. | Direct skill-build feed. Body or head, you win. |
| **Killer** | Crit kills → +36% Crit Damage for 10s | Your AR will be doing crits because the drone is ramping your Concerted Strike. |

Bridge AR (any T0): **Military 416 with Killer + Surgical** — Killer for crit damage, Surgical for headshot crit damage stacking. Less consistent than Adaptation but works at low gear levels.

<a id="combat-rotation"></a>

## Combat Rotation

1. **Pre-engage**: Deploy Striker Drone and Striker Hive in cover before the gunfight. Both skills start their duration and Concerted Strike begins stacking as soon as either hits an enemy.
2. **Open with AR fire** to start Play Rough's 25% DR. Stay on a single target for 2-3 seconds.
3. **Ceasefire briefly** (re-aim, reposition) — Play Rough drops the stored damage on the next enemy and your skill damage window opens.
4. **Drone Overload signature** when the room has 4+ enemies. +50% RoF and +100% SCD on drone for 12s = the burst window.
5. **Re-deploy Hive** when its duration runs out. Each re-deploy retriggers Drone Lock, retriggers Stabilized Flight's +6% SCC, and refreshes the EMCAT loop.
6. **Reset between fights**: Skill cooldowns refund cleanly via Engineering Core's SCR rolls. You should never be without a drone for more than 4-5 seconds.

## TL;DR — Five Things to Remember

1. **The loop is the build.** Striker Drone crit → EMCAT applies Disoriented → Stabilized Flight feeds back +6% SCC → next crit comes faster. Repeat for the whole fight.
2. **Two 3-piece skill mod sets at once.** EMCAT 3pc on the drone, Stabilized Flight 3pc on the hive. Most builds run a single 6-piece — this build splits the budget for the loop.
3. **Warlord's ceasefire bonus is the timing key.** Pause your AR briefly between volleys → you get +10% Skill Damage for 10s every cycle. Ceasefire is not a pause, it is a part of the rotation.
4. **Concerted Strike OS is +24% damage continuous.** Every time your drone or hive hits something within 20m, a 100% chance stack goes up. Three stacks always.
5. **Off-meta does not mean weak.** Jackpot 4pc + Deep Focus + Brainstorm gives this build top-end skill-crit numbers and a permanent CC field most Tech Op builds cannot match.
