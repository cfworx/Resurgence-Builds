## What it is

Do you want to win? Do you want to win so badly that the other team reports you for cheating because they literally cannot kill you? Look no further.

This is the dominant Conflict PVP build. Utilizing the **4pc Fearless Warrior** set, **2pc Phalanx Attack**, the **Warlord** exotic AR, and an MPX secondary, this build is designed to win 1v1s by simply refusing to die. Willspot went 24-0 in back-to-back matches with this. It is a certified spawn-trapping machine. 

## How it works (The Mathematics of Unfairness)

This build wins 1v1s by being **most defensive exactly when you're firing**:

1. **Fearless Warrior 4pc:** Flat +6% Damage Reduction, +9% Damage Bonus, +18% Toughness. It is the only set in the game that gives you tank AND damage simultaneously. It's greedy, and we love it. 
2. **Warlord's Play Rough (fixed exotic talent):** Firing reduces incoming damage by 25%. This stacks with Fearless Warrior's DR for a massive **29.5% damage reduction WHILE FIRING**. 
3. **Phalanx Attack 2pc:** +18% Magazine Size. Fewer reload windows means fewer windows of vulnerability. 
4. **Recycling Chest:** Taking damage within 20m has an 80% chance to refill 4.5 bullets. This sustains your fire indefinitely in a brawl. They shoot you, you get bullets back to shoot them. Fair trade.
5. **Explosive Fury Backpack:** Throwing a grenade grants +18% Rate of Fire for 10s, stacking twice for +36% RoF. You pre-throw this before engaging to turn your gun into a laser beam.

## Why it works

Most PVP builds force you to make a choice: do you want to do damage, or do you want to survive? This build laughs at tradeoffs and takes both. 

Play Rough's 25% DR is up whenever you're actively firing — which, in a PVP shooter, is 90% of the time. Combined with Fearless Warrior's flat damage bonus and toughness, you will out-TTK (time-to-kill) opponents who chose pure damage, and you will out-survive opponents who chose pure tank. You win by default. 

The MPX secondary with Steady Hands handles close-quarters for when some brave idiot gets inside the Warlord's effective range. Hip-firing grants +20% Stability and +15% Weapon Damage.

### Core split
4 Firepower + 2 Toughness. Put your damage on the Mask, Chest, Holster, and Gloves. Put your survival on the Backpack and Kneepads. 

## Rotation (How to go 24-0)

1. Pre-throw a grenade before engaging to get your Explosive Fury stack 1 (+18% Rate of Fire for 10s).
2. Throw a second grenade as the fight starts to get stack 2 (+36% RoF total). 
3. Open with the Warlord. Play Rough activates the very millisecond you fire, giving you 25% DR live. 
4. Let Recycling keep your mag topped off by absorbing incoming hits. 
5. Swap to the MPX if they get inside ~10m for hip-fire CQC dominance. 
6. Put your Shield up if you need to break a bad angle or if you accidentally took a fair fight.
