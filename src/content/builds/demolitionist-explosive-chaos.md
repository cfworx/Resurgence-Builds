# The Demolitionist — Explosive Chaos

## What is the most fun Demolitionist build in The Division Resurgence?

**The most fun Demolitionist build in The Division Resurgence pairs the Collateral Damage OS with the M32A1 Grenade Launcher signature so every skill cast triggers a free 5-meter explosion for 3,000% Engineering (~45,000 raw damage per proc at endgame). Layer Jackpot 4pc for +24% Skill Crit Damage, Explosive Burst backpack for up to +90% Skill Multi-Shot, and Lady Death exotic SMG (or a non-exotic Vector with In Sync + Multitasking) and the screen turns into a kill cam — every grenade throw can deal 200,000+ AoE damage on a crit + Multi-Shot proc.**

A **Demolitionist** build for The Division: Resurgence built around one core mechanic: **every Skill Damage event detonates twice.** Solo-first, math on the page, every entry verified verbatim against the spreadsheet.

## The Thesis — Why This Build Is the Most Fun in the Game

Most builds in Resurgence pick one damage source. This one **stacks four explosion events on every single skill cast**, and each one gets its own crit roll and its own Multi-Shot roll. Throw one M32A1 grenade and the screen does this:

1. The M32A1 grenade itself detonates — **+460% damage, +100% radius** from Thermobaric Grenades. That is one Skill Damage event.
2. That Skill Damage event triggers **Collateral Damage OS** — a second explosion within 5 meters of the target for **3,000% Engineering**. Second Skill Damage event.
3. The grenade throw triggers **Explosive Burst** backpack — **+9% Skill Multi-Shot for 2 seconds, up to 10 stacks (= up to +90%)**. Every additional explosion from now on rolls a Multi-Shot.
4. Each Multi-Shot proc on the M32A1 OR the Collateral Damage AoE OR the Artillery Turret OR the Seeker Mine triggers **another Skill Damage event**, which retriggers **Collateral Damage**.

> **One throw, four damage events minimum.** With Multi-Shot at full stacks, it's six or seven. With Skill Crits, the crit damage stack lifts each one another 24"“60%.

This is the most number-on-screen build in the game. Solo content turns into a music video.

### Why solo-first

- **Skill builds in solo never compete for aggro** — your Turret and Seeker Mine pull threat, you reposition, the M32A1 deletes a pack from outside their effective range.
- **No team-buff dependencies** — every multiplier in this build is self-fired. Nothing requires an ally trigger.
- **The signature M32A1 is one grenade per shot under Thermobaric**, so the resource pressure is high. You want full control of when it fires. Solo is where that control matters most.
- **The "fun" payoff is solo-shaped** — watching three packs evaporate from one grenade is what you came here for. Group content dilutes the kill credit.

## The Engine — Collateral Damage as the Multiplier on Every Skill

**Collateral Damage** (High-End Engineering OS) reads verbatim from the spreadsheet:

> *"When dealing Skill Damage, also deal damage equal to 3000% Engineering to enemies within 5 meters of the target. 10 seconds [cooldown]."*

This is the build's heart. Every skill in your loadout — Artillery Turret, Explosive Seeker Mine, M32A1 grenade — triggers a second, free 3,000% Engineering explosion on the target's location. The cooldown is 10 seconds **per Collateral Damage proc**, not per skill, so it cycles freely across the three skill streams.

### Why 3,000% Engineering is the biggest number on the page

Engineering is the Core attribute every gear slot in this build rolls. At a realistic endgame Engineering total of ~1,500, that's:

- **3,000% Engineering = 30 × 1,500 = 45,000 raw AoE damage per Collateral Damage trigger.**
- That's BEFORE Skill Intensity multiplies it (~1.4× with full Engineering bonus rolls).
- That's BEFORE the Skill Crit roll lands (+24% from Jackpot 4pc on crit).
- That's BEFORE Multi-Shot doubles or triples the event count.

**Total realistic per-trigger ceiling: ~200,000 damage in a 5-meter circle, free, attached to every M32A1 grenade and every Artillery Turret salvo.**

### Why Skill Damage builds beat Weapon Damage builds for chaos

In the Engineering bonus pool, the relevant stats are: **Skill Intensity, Skill Duration, Skill Cooldown Recovery, Skill Crit Chance, Skill Crit Damage, Skill Multi-Shot Chance, Skill Radius, Skill Blast / Piercing / Shredding.** Every one of these scales the explosion. The Weapon Damage pool (Crit Chance / Crit Damage / Headshot Damage / Multi-Shot) does not touch a single explosion. So you do not split rolls — every roll on every piece goes to one of three pools: **Skill Intensity, Skill Crit Chance, or Skill Crit Damage**. Nothing else.

## The Weapon — Lady Death (or the Non-Exotic Backup)

This build has a clear top-end weapon AND a clear non-exotic alternative. Not every player has the exotic — both paths are written into the build so you can run it from launch through endgame.

### Path 1 (BIS): Lady Death exotic SMG — "Breathe Free"

**Spreadsheet text (verbatim):** *"While moving, grants 4 stacks per second, or 8 stacks if sprinting, up to a maximum of 40 stacks. A stack is consumed with each round fired, granting +15% Weapon Damage. Deploying a Skill consumes 10 stacks and grants +15% Skill Damage. Kills grant +10% Movement Speed for 10 seconds."*

Lady Death is the only exotic in the spreadsheet (alongside Warlord AR and Strawberry Milkshake LMG) whose talent **directly converts skill deployment into Skill Damage**. The +15% Skill Damage refresh on every skill cast lands directly on every Collateral Damage trigger.

**How it feeds the build:**
1. Sprint between cover — generates 8 Breathe Free stacks/second, caps at 40 in 5 seconds.
2. Deploy Artillery Turret — consumes 10 stacks → +15% Skill Damage for the next skill window.
3. Deploy Explosive Seeker Mine — consumes another 10 stacks → +15% Skill Damage refreshed.
4. Fire M32A1 signature — every grenade and every Collateral Damage AoE benefits from +15% Skill Damage.
5. Shoot between casts — each round consumes 1 stack for +15% Weapon Damage. Kills refresh movement speed, which keeps you sprinting, which regenerates stacks.

### Path 2 (Non-exotic, equal in spirit): First Wave Vector 45 ACP T2 with In Sync + Multitasking

If you do not have Lady Death yet — or you do not chase exotics — chase a **T2 First Wave Vector 45 ACP** rolling **both In Sync AND Multitasking**. Confirmed in the spreadsheet weapon talent table: the Vector 45 ACP T2 is in the source pool for **both** talents, so a recalibrated roll can carry both at once.

**In Sync (verbatim):** *"Weapon Hits grant +9% Skill Damage for 8 seconds. Skill Hits grant +9% Weapon Damage for 8 seconds. Bonus is doubled when both buffs are active at the same time."*

**Multitasking (verbatim):** *"Dealing damage to 3 enemies within 5 seconds grants +30% Skill Critical Damage for 12 seconds. Talent Cooldown: 8 seconds."*

| Talent | Effect once active | Trigger | Uptime in this build |
|---|---|---|---|
| **In Sync** | **+18% Skill Damage / +18% Weapon Damage** (doubled when both buffs active) | Shoot the SMG while a skill is hitting — both trigger constantly | Permanent in any active fight |
| **Multitasking** | **+30% Skill Critical Damage** | Damage 3 enemies within 5s — trivial in solo packs | Permanent in any active fight |

**How this path compares to Lady Death:**

| Source | Skill Damage contribution | Other |
|---|---|---|
| Lady Death (Breathe Free) | +15% Skill Damage per skill cast (refreshed every cast) | +15% WD per round fired (40-stack pool) |
| Vector In Sync + Multitasking | **+18% Skill Damage** (doubled In Sync) + **+30% Skill Crit Damage** (Multitasking) | +18% Weapon Damage (doubled In Sync) |

**The Vector path actually trades roughly even with Lady Death on raw output**, with the upside of permanent +30% Skill Crit Damage on the AoE (Multitasking lands on every Collateral Damage trigger that hits 3+ enemies — which is most of them). Lady Death's edge is the movement-stack feel and the +15% movement speed on kills; the Vector path's edge is the +30% SCD permanent uptime once Multitasking is ramped.

**Other backups (lower priority):**

| Weapon | Talent | Why bench |
|---|---|---|
| **Modified USC (T2 SMG)** | Can roll In Sync | Solid In Sync chassis without Multitasking. Pair with any secondary that rolls Multitasking. |
| **Custom 416 G3 (T2 AR)** | Can roll Multitasking AND Multi-Pronged Attack | AR rather than SMG — slower fire rate, but Multi-Pronged Attack adds up to +18% Skill Multi-Shot Chance from hits. Decent if you prefer AR play. |
| **UIC15 MOD2 (T2 Rifle)** | Can roll In Sync, Multi-Pronged Attack, AND Long-Distance Marking | Best 3-talent stacker in the spreadsheet — if your recalibration can land any two of the three, it's a strong stat-stick primary. |
| **Warlord (Exotic AR)** | Play Rough — +10% Skill Damage for 10s when firing ceases | Defensive option. The +10% Skill Damage is real but requires a stop-firing trigger, breaking the rhythm. Pick Lady Death over Warlord. |

### Secondary slot: Stat-stick with In Sync

Regardless of which primary you run, the **secondary** slot is an In Sync stat-stick. If you run Lady Death primary, In Sync on the secondary means **every weapon hit (Lady Death) grants +9% Skill Damage and every skill hit grants +9% Weapon Damage**, doubled to +18% each when both are active. That's a permanent passive even when the secondary is holstered, because In Sync's "Weapon Hits" trigger is reading from the equipped primary's hits — you do not need to fire the secondary itself. Hold any AR or SMG with In Sync as the secondary; you will never draw it.

If you run the Vector with In Sync + Multitasking as your primary, **swap the In Sync description on the secondary for something else** — common picks: a weapon with **Slaughter Fortitude** (+12% Armor on kills/assists, 3 stacks) for solo survival, or **Conceited** (+15% DR on kills/assists). The passive lives on the holstered weapon.

## Per-Piece Stat Sheet — Every Slot, Every Stat

Every gear piece rolls **one Core Attribute** (Firepower / Toughness / Engineering) plus **two random Bonus Attributes** from the matching pool. The Core determines the bonus pool you can roll. **Engineering Core on every piece** — non-negotiable.

| Slot | Core | Bonus #1 | Bonus #2 | Brand / Talent |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mask | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Crit Chance | Jackpot |
| Chest | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Crit Damage | **T2 Ops Armor with Energize** |
| Backpack | Engineering | Skill Intensity | Skill Multi-Shot Chance | **T2 AMP24 with Explosive Burst** |
| Gloves | Engineering | Skill Crit Chance | Skill Crit Damage | Jackpot |
| Holster | Engineering | Skill Crit Damage | Skill Intensity | Jackpot |
| Knee Pads | Engineering | Skill Crit Chance | Skill Crit Damage | Jackpot |

### Why those exact stats

- **Skill Intensity (3 pieces)** — multiplies the base damage of every skill hit AND the Collateral Damage explosion. Largest single multiplier in the Engineering pool. Stack on Mask / Chest / Holster.
- **Skill Crit Chance (3 pieces)** — Jackpot 3pc already gives +10.8%; adding bonus rolls pushes the build toward ~25% sustained crit chance. Every crit fires Jackpot's +24% SCD.
- **Skill Crit Damage (4 pieces)** — Jackpot 4pc gives +24% baseline. Bonus rolls of 4"“6% per piece push the multiplier on each crit toward ×1.55"“1.70 — and that multiplier hits the 3,000% Engineering AoE every time.
- **Skill Multi-Shot Chance (1 piece, backpack only)** — Explosive Burst already provides up to +90% from grenades; one bonus-roll piece is enough.

### Stats you do NOT chase (reroll on sight)

- **Skill Duration** — this build does not benefit. M32A1 is one-shot, ESM detonates instantly, Turret has its own duration extension via Optimized mod set if you want it. Skill Duration is a healer stat, not a chaos stat.
- **Skill Cooldown Recovery** — modest value here. ESM cooldown is short, Turret has its own CDR via Cooling System, M32A1 is a signature with its own charge timer. SCDR rolls do not survive vs. SCD / SCC.
- **All Firepower bonus stats** — Weapon Damage, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Headshot Damage, Multi-Shot. Wrong Core entirely.
- **Skill Radius bonus rolls** — Jackpot 2pc already gives +12%. M32A1 Thermobaric already gives +100%. Saturated.
- **Received Healing / Damage to Elites** — fine in a vacuum, but not in the top 4 priorities. Reroll into Skill Intensity.

## The Math — Per Explosion, Per Cycle

Scenario: solo player, mid-mission, full Engineering Core endgame loadout. Engineering total ≈ **1,500** (6 gear pieces × ~150 Engineering Core average + Collateral Damage OS +97 Engineering rating + bonus rolls).

### One M32A1 grenade cycle, ramped (5 seconds in)

| Step | Source | Effect | Running damage on the target |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Grenade throw | M32A1 signature + Thermobaric mod | One grenade, +100% radius, **+460% damage** over base | grenade hit |
| 2. Collateral Damage triggers | OS — Skill Damage event detected | **3,000% Engineering** = 30 × 1,500 = **45,000** raw AoE to all enemies within 5m of the target | **+45,000** |
| 3. Skill Intensity multiplies the AoE | ~+40% SI from 3 bonus rolls + Concentrated Fire conditional | × 1.40 (conservative) | **45,000 × 1.40 = 63,000** |
| 4. Crit roll on the AoE | ~25% Skill Crit Chance (Jackpot 3pc + bonus rolls) | × 1.24 (+24% SCD Jackpot 4pc) when it crits | **on crit: 63,000 × 1.24 = ~78,000** |
| 5. Skill Crit Damage ramp from Area Suppression | Turret hits on 2+ ramping +2% SCD × 5 stacks = +10% | × 1.10 | **on crit, ramped: ~85,800** |
| 6. Energize active (used Medkit pre-fight) | +18% Skill Damage refreshed by every Multi-Shot proc | × 1.18 | **~101,200** |
| 7. Multi-Shot roll | Explosive Burst ~50% (5 stacks) + Fragmentation Charge +6.4% + Frag Grenades +5% = ~61% MS | ~61% chance the explosion fires a SECOND time at full damage | **on MS proc: +~101,200 (second event)** |

**Per Collateral Damage trigger, on a crit + Multi-Shot, ramped:** ~**200,000+ damage** in a 5-meter circle, on top of the grenade's own damage.

### If you are running the non-exotic Vector path

Substitute Lady Death's +15% Skill Damage (per cast) for **In Sync's +18% Skill Damage** (doubled, permanent uptime) and **Multitasking's +30% Skill Crit Damage** on the AoE. The non-exotic line works out to:

- Step 6 replacement: Energize +18% × **In Sync +18% Skill Damage** = × 1.18 × 1.18 → **~119,400** before MS
- Step 5 addition: **Multitasking +30% Skill Crit Damage** stacks on top of Jackpot 4pc +24% and Area Suppression +10% → **+30% SCD additional** on crits
- **Per Collateral Damage trigger on a crit + MS with Vector path:** ~**210,000+ damage** — slightly higher than Lady Death's peak, lower steady-state because In Sync requires both weapon + skill hits to land for the double bonus

**Both paths deliver the same fundamental experience.** Lady Death feels better; Vector benches it slightly on raw math. Run whichever you have.

|---|---|---|
| Jackpot 3pc | +10.8% | — |
| Jackpot 4pc | — | +24.0% |

That means **~1-in-4 explosions land at 1.55× damage (Lady Death) or 1.85× damage (Vector)**. The Vector path actually wins on crit damage ceiling because of Multitasking. Lady Death wins on the movement-feed feel.

## Skill Mod Choices — Locked

### M32A1 Grenade Launcher: Thermobaric Grenades (3-piece)

**Spreadsheet text:** *"Reduces Grenades in Grenade Launcher to 1, expands the Explosion Radius by 100% and increases damage by 460%."*

- **Why:** One grenade, one obliteration. The +460% damage means the grenade itself is a one-shot delete on most non-elite packs. The +100% radius means it covers any room you're aiming into. The single-grenade restriction is a feature — it forces deliberate target selection and prevents you from wasting Collateral Damage's 10-second cooldown on overlapping triggers.
- **Bench mods:** **Concentrated Fire** (5 M32A1 hits in 3s = +14.4% Skill Intensity for 15s) — does not fire under Thermobaric (1 grenade per charge). **Airburst** is a backup if Thermobaric is out of reach; the +6% SCD per hit on 1-enemy is decent but does not compete with Thermobaric's raw output.

### Artillery Turret: Area Suppression (3-piece)

**Spreadsheet text:** *"Artillery Turret hits on up to 1 enemies have a 50% chance of granting +2% Skill Critical Damage for 10 seconds, up to 5 stacks. Hits on more than 1 enemies have a guaranteed chance of granting the bonus."*

- **Why:** The Turret reliably hits multiple enemies in solo play because you drop it into packs. **The guaranteed +2% per hit on 2+ enemies means full +10% SCD ramps in 3"“4 seconds.**
- **Bench mods:** **Cooling System** (Skill Crits reduce Turret cooldown by 1.20%, 1s talent CD) for a uptime-focused alternative. **Frag Grenades** (+1% Skill Multi-Shot per Turret hit, 5 stacks = +5%) — Area Suppression beats it for crit ramp. **Optimized** (+8% Turret RoF, +0.5s duration per hit on 2+ enemies, capped 6s extra) for boss content where Turret duration matters.

### Explosive Seeker Mine: Multi-Directional Charge (3-piece)

**Spreadsheet text:** *"Explosive Seeker Mine hits on enemies afflicted by Bleed or Vulnerable apply the same debuff to all enemies within 10 meter(s) and grant +7.20% Firepower for 10 second(s)."*

- **Why:** Artillery Turret applies Bleed naturally through Frag debris. The Seeker Mine homes onto a Bleeding enemy and **spreads the Bleed to every enemy within 10 meters**, lighting up the whole pack as another Skill Damage tick source. The +7.20% Firepower feeds your secondary weapon talent stacks.
- **Bench mods:** **Cooling Device** (ESM Crits reduce ESM cooldown by 10%, 3s talent CD) for a CDR-focused alternative. **Applied Demolitions** (ESM hits on 1 enemy grant +3.20% SCD for 20s, 3 stacks = +9.6%). **Fragmentation Charge** (ESM hits grant +6.40% Skill Multi-Shot for 10s) — Explosive Burst already saturates Multi-Shot.

### OS Protocol: Collateral Damage (Engineering, High-End)

**Spreadsheet text:** *"When dealing Skill Damage, also deal damage equal to 3000% Engineering to enemies within 5 meters of the target. 10 seconds [cooldown]."*

- **Why:** This is the entire build. Every other choice exists to feed Collateral Damage.
- **Bench OS:** **Explosive Charge** (HE Engineering) — *"Shooting for 3 seconds enhances the next Skill for 8 seconds. Dealing damage with the Enhanced Skill creates an explosion with a radius of 5 meters around it that deals damage equal to 1600% Engineering. 5 seconds cooldown."* Roughly half the per-trigger damage but a shorter cooldown. **Surgical Engineering** (HE Engineering, +15% SI passive) — *"Skill Critical Hits have a 60% chance of granting +16% Skill Critical Damage for 10 seconds, up to 3 stacks."* (=+48% SCD potential). Bench OS for SCD-focused playstyle if you do not have Collateral Damage yet.

## Why Energize Earns the Chest Slot

**Spreadsheet text:** *"Using a Medical Kit grants +18% Skill Damage for 8 seconds. Triggering a Skill Multi-Shot refreshes the duration."*

The trick: **Triggering a Skill Multi-Shot refreshes the duration.** This build generates Skill Multi-Shot procs constantly:

- **Explosive Burst backpack:** Every grenade throw = +9% Skill Multi-Shot for 2s, up to 10 stacks. The signature M32A1 is a grenade throw. Every regular grenade you throw is a grenade throw.
- **Fragmentation Charge** (ESM mod): +6.40% Skill Multi-Shot for 10s per ESM hit.
- **Frag Grenades** (Turret mod): +1% Skill Multi-Shot per Turret hit, 5 stacks = +5%.

With ~25"“60% sustained Skill Multi-Shot Chance and 3"“5 skill events per cycle, you trigger a Skill Multi-Shot **every 1"“2 seconds in any fight**. Energize's 8-second duration **refreshes constantly** — the +18% Skill Damage is effectively permanent uptime.

### Why not the alternatives

| Chest talent | Effect (verbatim) | Why not |
|---|---|---|
| **Energize** | "+18% Skill Damage for 8s. Skill Multi-Shot refreshes duration." | **PICK.** Permanent +18% Skill Damage in any active fight. |
| Territory Expansion | "+30% Skill Radius while at full Health" | Conditional on full HP — fragile in solo, and Jackpot 2pc + Thermobaric already saturate radius. |
| Glass Cannon | "+20% Damage, -10% DR" | +20% Damage (not Skill Damage specifically) but the -10% DR is brutal in solo where you're the only target. Bench option. |
| Toymaker | "+15% Skill Multi-Shot when no enemies within 20m" | The 20m rule is harsh for a Demolitionist who plays at mid-range. Explosive Burst saturates Multi-Shot already. |
| Crossfire | "+15% WD when Skills deployed" | Weapon Damage only — does not touch the Skill Damage half of the build. |

**Energize is the only chest talent that turns the wearer's slot into a permanent Skill Damage multiplier on the Collateral Damage explosion.**

## Why Explosive Burst Is the Only Backpack

**Spreadsheet text:** *"Throwing a Grenade grants +9% Skill Multi-shot Chance for 2 seconds, up to 10 stacks. Talent Cooldown: 3 seconds."*

The math: **+9% × 10 stacks = +90% Skill Multi-Shot Chance** at full ramp. A Skill Multi-Shot doubles a Skill Damage event. **Every Collateral Damage explosion gets a ~50"“90% chance to fire twice** at full Explosive Burst stacks.

### Why grenade-throw-triggered works for a Demolitionist

You have **two grenade sources** in this build:

1. **Regular fragmentation grenades** (the in-game grenade button) — throw one between skill casts to ramp Explosive Burst.
2. **M32A1 signature grenade** — counts as a grenade throw, banks 1 stack of Explosive Burst per signature cycle.

You ramp Explosive Burst to 5+ stacks pre-engagement just by throwing one frag while moving into position. By the time the M32A1 fires, you're at 6 stacks (+54% Multi-Shot) and climbing.

### Why not the alternatives

| Backpack talent | Effect (verbatim) | Why not |
|---|---|---|
| **Explosive Burst** | "+9% Skill MS / grenade throw, up to 10 stacks (+90%). 3s talent CD." | **PICK.** Pairs with Energize's Multi-Shot refresh trigger and ramps Multi-Shot to ceiling. |
| Minion Training | "Skill Crit Kills grant +36% SCD for 12s" | Great Skill Crit Damage source — but triggers on Skill Crit *Kill*, not every crit. Realistically only fires after the second or third skill event. Explosive Burst's MS uptime is more consistent. |
| Intense Performance | "+33% Skill Radius, -5% Skill Duration" | Radius is already saturated (Jackpot 2pc + Thermobaric +100%). Small trade either way. |
| Inspiration | "+30% RoF when Skills deployed" | Feeds Lady Death's stack burn rate but not the Skill Damage half. |
| Overwatch | "+15% Skill Damage when behind cover 3s+" | Forces 3-second cover holds — Demolitionists move between throws. Never reaches its prerequisite. |
| Meditation | "+3.6% SCC per second immobile, 5 stacks (+18%)" | Requires immobility — conflicts with Lady Death's Breathe Free movement-stack mechanic. Pick one or the other. |
| Explosive Fury | "+18% RoF / grenade throw, up to 2 stacks (+36%)" | Lady Death already runs at 900 RPM. RoF over MS is the wrong trade. |

## Combat Rotation — Solo

You are not pressing one button. You are **positioning Lady Death's stack pool (or Vector's In Sync uptime), opening with the right skill, and letting the chain reaction handle the rest**.

### Pre-pull (5 seconds before contact)

1. **Use a Medical Kit.** Energize fires — +18% Skill Damage for 8 seconds. The Multi-Shot procs in the next 8 seconds will refresh duration indefinitely.
2. **Sprint into position.** Lady Death's Breathe Free generates 8 stacks/second sprinting. In 5 seconds you cap at 40 stacks. (Vector path: just keep moving toward cover.)
3. **Throw a regular grenade at the pack's anticipated spawn.** Explosive Burst stacks ×1 = +9% MS. The grenade lands, applies its own damage, and Collateral Damage's 10s cooldown is now ARMED for the next Skill Damage event.

### Opening salvo (seconds 0"“6)

4. **Drop Artillery Turret** at the pack's flank. (Lady Death path: consumes 10 stacks → +15% Skill Damage for 10s.) **Turret salvo lands → Collateral Damage triggers → 5m explosion = 3,000% Engineering on each hit target.** Area Suppression starts ramping (+2% SCD per hit on 2+ enemies, up to +10%). Each Turret hit applies fragment Bleed.
5. **Deploy Explosive Seeker Mine** at the highest-HP enemy in the pack. (Lady Death path: another 10 stacks consumed → +15% Skill Damage refreshed.) **ESM homes a Bleeding enemy → Multi-Directional Charge spreads Bleed to all enemies within 10m.**
6. **Fire M32A1 signature** at the densest pack center. Thermobaric mod = 1 grenade, +100% radius, +460% damage. **Grenade lands → another Skill Damage event → Collateral Damage fires.** Energize duration refreshes via the next Multi-Shot proc.

### Sustained chaos (seconds 6"“30)

7. **Shoot your primary between skill casts.** (Lady Death: each round consumes 1 Breathe Free stack for +15% WD. Kills grant +10% MS for 10s — keep sprinting between covers to maintain the stack pool. Vector: hold In Sync uptime by keeping at least one weapon hit landing every 8 seconds while skills are also hitting; Multitasking ramps the moment you've damaged 3 enemies in 5s — trivial in any pack.)
8. **Re-deploy Artillery Turret the instant its CD pops.** Cooling System mod (if running) gives 1.20% CD reduction per skill crit, 1s talent CD. With ~25% Skill Crit Chance and 5+ skill events per cycle, Turret is back online in 8"“12 seconds.
9. **Re-throw a regular grenade every ~5 seconds.** Explosive Burst stacks climb from 5 back to 10. Each grenade throw is also an Energize Multi-Shot refresh window (next MS proc inside 8s refreshes Energize).
10. **Save the M32A1 signature for elite packs.** It is one grenade per charge — use it on the highest-priority target group.

### When you get focused (single elite or boss)

- **Use a Medical Kit.** Energize refreshes. Health restored.
- **Drop Seeker Mine on the elite directly** — it tracks. Multi-Directional Charge fires its Bleed/Vuln spread on the boss's adds. Collateral Damage AoE clips the elite for 45,000+ raw Engineering damage.
- **Reposition behind cover.** Lady Death's Breathe Free stacks rebuild while sprinting. Wait for skill cooldowns.
- **Fire the M32A1 only on overlapping packs.** Single-target on a boss wastes the +100% radius.

### What you do NOT need to do

- **You do not chase headshots.** Neither Lady Death's Breathe Free nor In Sync require headshot triggers — every body shot counts.
- **You do not coordinate with allies.** No talent in this build requires an ally trigger.
- **You do not stand in cover for 3+ seconds.** Overwatch is not picked. You sprint between cover constantly.

> **The rotation feels like a fireworks show.** Every 3"“5 seconds another Collateral Damage explosion paints the screen. By the third cycle, you have killed everything in a 30-meter radius and you are still moving.

## The Multi-Shot Cascade — Why The Build Snowballs

Skill Multi-Shot Chance is the secret crit-stacking mechanic of this build. Here is what feeds it:

| Source | Multi-Shot Chance | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Explosive Burst backpack | Up to **+90%** | +9% per grenade throw, 10 stacks, 2s duration each. Refreshed by every grenade. |
| Fragmentation Charge (ESM mod, alt) | +6.40% | ESM hits grant for 10s |
| Frag Grenades (Turret mod, alt) | +5% (5 stacks × 1%) | Per Turret damage event |
| Multi-Pronged Attack (weapon talent, alt) | Up to +18% (5 stacks × 3.6%) | Per hit, missed hits remove 1 stack |
| Fire Cycle 4pc (bridge set) | +12% | Passive set bonus |
| **Total realistic mid-fight** | **~70%** (Explosive Burst 5 stacks + Fragmentation Charge + Frag Grenades) | After 2 grenade throws + 1 ESM cast + 5 Turret hits |
| **Total ramped peak** | **~110%** (Explosive Burst 10 stacks + all other sources) | After 5 grenade throws sustained |

### What that does to a Collateral Damage trigger

A Multi-Shot proc doubles a Skill Damage event. **At ~70% Multi-Shot, 70% of Collateral Damage triggers fire twice.** That's not 1.7× expected damage — it's a coin flip where heads = double damage and tails = base damage, weighted toward heads.

Expected damage per Collateral Damage trigger (Lady Death path):

- **Base case:** 45,000 Engineering AoE × 1.40 SI × 1.18 Energize = **74,340 damage**
- **With crit (25% chance):** 74,340 × 1.24 Jackpot SCD = **92,182 damage**
- **With Multi-Shot proc (70% chance):** 74,340 × 2 = **148,680 damage**
- **With crit AND Multi-Shot (17.5% chance):** 92,182 × 2 = **184,364 damage**

**Expected value per Collateral Damage trigger** (weighted average): **~115,000 damage in a 5-meter circle, every skill hit.**

**Vector path adds Multitasking's +30% SCD on crits → ~125,000 expected value per trigger.**

In a 30-second fight you fire ~3 Collateral Damage triggers = **~345,000 free AoE damage** on top of the base skill damage.

## TL;DR — The Five Things to Remember

### 1. One OS. Collateral Damage.
**Every Skill Damage hit triggers a 5-meter AoE for 3,000% Engineering.** At ~1,500 Engineering endgame, that's a **45,000-damage base explosion**, free, attached to every Turret salvo / Seeker Mine / M32A1 grenade. **10-second cooldown per proc.** This is the build.

### 2. M32A1 Thermobaric + Artillery Turret Area Suppression + ESM Multi-Directional Charge.
**M32A1: 1 grenade, +100% radius, +460% damage.** One nuke per signature charge. **Turret Area Suppression: +2% Skill Crit Damage guaranteed per hit on 2+ enemies, 5 stacks (+10% SCD).** **ESM Multi-Directional Charge: spreads Bleed/Vuln to all enemies in 10m + +7.20% Firepower.** Three skills, three independent Skill Damage event streams, all feeding Collateral Damage.

### 3. Lady Death OR the Vector backup.
**Lady Death exotic SMG (BIS).** Breathe Free generates 4 stacks/sec moving, 8/sec sprinting (cap 40). Each round burns 1 stack for +15% WD. **Deploying a skill consumes 10 stacks for +15% Skill Damage.** **Non-exotic backup: First Wave Vector 45 ACP T2 with In Sync + Multitasking.** Confirmed in the spreadsheet — the Vector 45 ACP T2 is in the source pool for both talents simultaneously. +18% Skill Damage doubled (In Sync) + +30% Skill Crit Damage permanent uptime (Multitasking). Equal-in-spirit to Lady Death on raw math.

### 4. Jackpot 4pc + Energize chest + Explosive Burst backpack.
**Engineering Core on every gear slot.** Top-3 stats: **Skill Intensity → Skill Crit Chance → Skill Crit Damage.** **Jackpot 4pc: +10.8% Skill Crit Chance + +24% Skill Crit Damage.** **Energize chest: +18% Skill Damage permanent uptime via Multi-Shot refresh.** **Explosive Burst backpack: +9% Skill Multi-Shot per grenade throw, up to +90%.** **Bridge to Fire Cycle 4pc** for an extra +12% Skill Multi-Shot until you complete Jackpot.

### 5. The numbers to remember.
**One Collateral Damage explosion = ~45,000 raw → ~74,000 with Skill Intensity + Energize → ~92,000 on a crit → ~185,000 on a crit + Multi-Shot.** **Expected value per trigger: ~115,000 AoE damage** (Lady Death path) / **~125,000** (Vector path with Multitasking). **3 triggers per 30-second fight = ~345,000 free AoE damage** on top of the base skill damage. **Every grenade throw stacks +9% Multi-Shot, refreshing Energize, priming the next Collateral Damage cycle.** **The screen turns into a kill cam.**
