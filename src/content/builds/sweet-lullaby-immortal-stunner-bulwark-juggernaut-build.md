Every now and then a community submission lands that does not need polish, just a stage. This is one of those. Nadie sent over the Immortal Stunner build, constructed around a golden **Pummeling Shield** OS Protocol. The brief: "find a safe corner, stun the guy with longest health bar and throw some snacks for my boys." Hard to argue with that gameplay loop.

This is a **stun-tank archetype**. You are sitting at **22,610 Power Score, 60,260 DPS, 13,216 Armor, and 336,081 Health**. The 4pc Healing Elites gives you received healing, extra protection, and armor stacking. The 2pc Self-Propelled Shield gives additional armor and mobility. The Pummeling Shield OS Protocol stuns anything you can keep hitting while you have Extra Health. Unflinching on the body armor restores health based on Toughness every time you take damage. Rock Solid on the backpack gives damage reduction every time you receive healing. The core loop is: get hit, heal, get tougher, spray, stun, repeat. This is not a glass cannon. It is not a DPS poster boy. It is the immovable object that turns elite encounters into a calm afternoon at the petting zoo.

## How the Weapons Feed the Stun Loop

The weapon selections are not random. Each one exists to trigger and maintain the Pummeling Shield stun condition.

### Primary: PPSH-41

**Backup Protection:** "Swapping to this weapon grants Extra Health equal to 20% Max Health for 8 seconds."

Every time Nadie swaps to the PPSH, they get a chunk of Extra Health. That is the exact condition the Pummeling Shield needs to start stunning. Swap, get Extra Health, start spraying, stack hits, trigger stuns. 900 RPM with a 71-round magazine means hits stack fast. The Pummeling Shield stuns every 10 hits while you have Extra Health, so a single PPSH magazine is multiple stuns worth of work.

**Damage Trade:** "+8.40% Weapon Damage for each 20% of the Magazine Size emptied, up to 4 stacks." With a 71-round mag, the damage ramps as the magazine drains. By the time you are hitting the last 15 rounds, you are swinging with meaningful bonus damage on top of the stuns.

### Secondary: FAMAS

**Swift Taunt:** "Hitting 3 enemies within 5 seconds taunts nearby enemies and grants Extra Health equal to 35% Max Health for 10 seconds."

This is the second Extra Health source. Tag three enemies with the FAMAS, pull aggro, and get 35% Max Health as Extra Health. That is another Pummeling Shield trigger. The taunt effect also means enemies focus you instead of your squad, which is exactly what a tank wants.

**Shooting Repair:** "Shooting 10 bullets restores 2.50% Missing Health of your deployed Skills." Keeps your Phalanx Shield and Shockwave Spike topped off without dipping into skill rotations.

## Why This Build Works

1. **Multiple Extra Health triggers.** Pummeling Shield needs Extra Health to start stunning. Backup Protection, Swift Taunt, and the Healing Elites set effects all feed Extra Health. You are almost never without it.
2. **Toughness and Health stacking.** The Pummeling Shield itself adds +45% Toughness, +447 Toughness, and +11,753 Health. Combined with the rest of the gear, you sit at 336k Health with 13.2k Armor. The build does not just survive, it laughs at incoming damage.
3. **Damage feedback loop.** Unflinching restores Health based on Toughness. Rock Solid adds Damage Reduction when you receive healing. These two stack into a self-perpetuating defensive cycle that gets stronger the more fire you take.
4. **Group utility.** Stuns are not just self-defense. Stunning the elite means your squad takes less return fire. The tank role here meaningfully buffs the group's effective DPS, not just soaks damage.
5. **No exotics required.** Both weapons are standard T2 legendary drops. The entire loadout is farmable gear, which makes this build realistic to put together.

## Honest Critique

**What is brilliant:** The triple Extra Health feed (Backup Protection on weapon swap, Swift Taunt on FAMAS, set bonuses) means you basically never lose the Pummeling Shield stun condition. That is genuinely smart kit synergy.

**What is worth knowing:** 60.26k DPS with 14.36% crit chance and 50% crit damage means you are leaving offensive potential on the table compared to dedicated damage Specializations. That is on purpose. This build does not want to be the top damage dealer. It wants to be the reason the top damage dealer is still alive.

**Where it shines:** Group play. Elite missions. Dark Zone. Anywhere you have a squad to provide the damage output while you provide the immortality and the stuns. This is a squad-anchor build.

**The cheeky observation:** Naming it "Sweet Lullaby" is on theme. You are literally putting elites to sleep with stuns. Whether the petting-zoo analogy or the lullaby metaphor sticks first, both work. Your squad is just standing there unloading into a stunned boss while you hold aggro and refuse to die. Main character energy, except the main character chose to be the bouncer.
