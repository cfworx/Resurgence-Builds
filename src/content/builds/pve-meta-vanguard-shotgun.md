## What is the meta PVE build in The Division: Resurgence right now?

The current meta PVE build is a **Vanguard** loadout running **4pc Falen Attack + 2pc Gunny Johnny**, a **T2 Double Barrel Shotgun** with **Steady Reload + Thick Skin**, **Scanning Pulse**, **Commando Smart Cover**, and the **Explosive Round** OS Protocol. The build was popularized by [Godlike](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cLrWBvz3F64), who calls it "the premier DPS build in the game when it comes to firearms" and "the best PVE build in the game — you can use it for speedrunning." This guide documents that build with the math on the page so you can replicate it, recalibrate it, and understand why every piece is where it is.

A **Vanguard** build for The Division: Resurgence built around **per-shot multi-shot scaling, Pulse vulnerability, and Explosive Round critical detonations**. Meta, spreadsheet-verified, math on the page.

**At a glance:**

- **Specialization:** Vanguard — **Tactical Link signature**
- **Skills:** **Scanning Pulse** + Optimal Awareness mod set (3pc) · **Commando Smart Cover** + Armored Train mod set (5pc)
- **Primary weapon:** **Double Barrel Shotgun** (T2) with **Steady Reload + Thick Skin**
- **Secondary weapon:** **FAMAS** (T2 AR)
- **Chest / Backpack:** **Duality** · **Street Fighter**
- **OS Protocol:** **Explosive Round** (Firepower, High-End — crits while aiming detonate for 2,000% firepower in 5m)
- **Gear set:** **4pc Falen Attack** (Strike Fury) + **2pc Gunny Johnny** (T2 from Legendary)
- **Stat priority:** Weapon Crit Chance → Weapon Crit Damage → Multi-Shot → Weapon Damage
- **Gear Core:** Firepower on every slot

> **Important:** This build is **extremely squishy** — base stats are pure damage. It requires a Bulwark teammate to draw aggro, or extremely careful positioning. See the [Combat Rotation](#combat-rotation) section.

## The Thesis — Why Vanguard + Shotgun?

The Vanguard spec is the highest-ceiling DPS spec in the game right now, and the reason is **Scanning Pulse stacking**. Every pulsed enemy takes **+25% Weapon Damage and +5% Vulnerable Damage** from your shots, and the Vanguard 3pc Optimal Awareness mod adds **up to +8% Weapon Crit Chance** to pulsed targets within 70m. That is a free **+25% damage layer** on every single shot you fire at a pulsed enemy.

The Falen Attack (Strike Fury) gear set then layers on:
- **2pc:** +18% Magazine Size
- **3pc:** +18% Rate of Fire
- **4pc:** +12% Multi-Shot

The +12% Multi-Shot is the killer — it means **12% of your shots fire an extra bullet**, and with the Double Barrel firing two shells per trigger pull, every Multi-Shot proc is effectively a third or fourth pellet hit at full damage.

Add **Explosive Round** OS Protocol on top — every crit while aiming detonates a **5m radius explosion for 2,000% firepower damage** — and a single aimed crit with a shotgun becomes a small AOE bomb. With Falen 4pc's Multi-Shot, you can trigger the Explosive Round procs **multiple times per trigger pull**.

> Build philosophy: this is a **three-engine stack** — Pulse multiplier × Falen Multi-Shot × Explosive Round AOE. Each engine multiplies the others. Strip any one and the build loses 30-40% of its ceiling.

## The Weapons — Double Barrel + FAMAS

### Primary: Double Barrel Shotgun (T2)

The Double Barrel is the highest per-trigger damage weapon in the game. Two shells fire on every trigger pull, and with Falen 4pc Multi-Shot rolling on top, you frequently get 3-4 pellet groups per pull.

**Talents:**
- **Steady Reload (or Steady Reload 2):** Reloading from empty grants weapon damage for a duration. T2 version is stronger.
- **Thick Skin:** Damage reduction while firing.

**Weapon mods:**
- Magazine: Reload Speed (+16% reload)
- Firepower mods (×2)
- Weapon Blast Damage attribute
- Accuracy attribute
- Optimal Range +7.5%

The Double Barrel is the win condition. Once you're inside its effective range (≤15m), you delete elites in 1-2 shots.

### Secondary: FAMAS (T2 AR)

The FAMAS is the bridge weapon for when you cannot get into shotgun range. Use it for kiting, sniping skill drones, finishing low-HP targets at 25-40m, and breaking armor before you close the gap.

If you do not have T2 FAMAS yet, **any AR with Killer + Long-Distance Marking** or **any T1 AR with similar burst talents** will work as the secondary.

### Tactical Augmentation (Free Event Reward)

There is a free **Shotgun Augmentation** from the recent in-game event that provides **+40% damage** to shotguns. Apply it. It is currently bugged in the patch notes but the buff is live.

## The Math — One Pulsed Shotgun Crit, End-to-End

Assume:
- Base Double Barrel shell damage: 100 (normalized for math)
- Two shells per trigger pull = 200 base
- Weapon Crit Chance: 60% (rolled from gear + Vanguard 3pc on pulsed target)
- Weapon Crit Damage: +180% (from cores, bonuses, Gunny 2pc)
- Multi-Shot Chance: +24% (Falen 4pc 12% + 1 Multi-Shot bonus stat at 8% + 1 more at 4%)
- Pulsed target: +25% Weapon Damage
- Falen 3pc: +18% Rate of Fire (more trigger pulls per second)
- Tactical Augmentation: +40% Shotgun Damage
- Tactical Link signature active: +20% Crit Chance, free lock-on

### Per-trigger calculation (one pulled shotgun trigger pull, on a pulsed target):

**Step 1 — Base + tactical aug:**
`200 × 1.40 = 280`

**Step 2 — Pulsed bonus:**
`280 × 1.25 = 350`

**Step 3 — Crit multiplier (60% chance × 280% damage):**
On crit: `350 × 2.80 = 980`
Average: `350 × (0.40 + 0.60 × 2.80) = 350 × 2.08 = 728`

**Step 4 — Multi-Shot proc (24% chance of an extra full shell pair):**
Expected extra damage: `728 × 0.24 = 175`
**Total per trigger pull (average): 728 + 175 = ~903**

### Now add Explosive Round (HE Firepower OS):

When you crit while aiming (60% × ADS uptime, say 80%):
- **Trigger chance per pull:** ~48%
- **Explosive Round damage:** 2,000% firepower damage in 5m

With Vanguard's firepower cores stacked, your firepower stat is ~900-1100 at endgame. 2,000% of that is **18,000-22,000 damage in a 5m radius** — applied to **every enemy in that radius**, not just the one you shot.

**On crowded packs, the Explosive Round AOE often outdamages the base shell hits 2-to-1.**

### Tactical Link signature window (15 seconds):

Inside Tactical Link:
- +20% Weapon Crit Chance → effective crit chance hits 80%
- Auto-lock-on means every shot hits center mass
- Average per-trigger jumps from ~903 to **~1,180+**
- Across a 15-second window at 18% RoF boost from Falen 3pc, that is **~25+ trigger pulls = 30,000+ raw damage plus the Explosive Round AOE per crit**

This is why Godlike calls it "astronomical damage."

## Skill Mod Choices — Optimal Awareness + Armored Train

### Scanning Pulse → Optimal Awareness (3pc)
- **2pc:** Bonus Armor
- **3pc:** Pulsed enemies within 70m grant **+1.4% to +4% Weapon Crit Chance** (scales up to +8% max)

This is the engine that justifies the entire build. Pulse 8 targets at once, sit at 60% base crit, hit 68%+ crit chance on every shot.

### Commando Smart Cover → Armored Train (5pc)
- **5pc:** Bonus Armor + **5% chance on weapon hits to grant Weapon Crit Chance**

Smart Cover itself provides **40% damage reduction** to anyone behind it — that is your survival layer. Drop it before peeking, then fire from behind cover for the DR.

**Important:** Smart Cover scales with **Engineering**. With Firepower cores, you won't have much Engineering, so the DR percentage will be at its base value (40%). That is still huge.

## Why Explosive Round Earns the OS Slot

Explosive Round is a **High-End Firepower OS Protocol** that reads, verbatim:

> *"Critical hits performed while aiming detonate a 5m radius explosion dealing 2,000% Firepower damage."*

The math:
- Vanguard endgame Firepower stat: 900-1100
- 2,000% of that: **18,000-22,000 damage per detonation**
- Detonations stack with your shell damage (separate damage instance)
- The detonation hits **everyone in 5m**, not just the target
- Falen 4pc Multi-Shot can proc **multiple Explosive Round detonations per trigger pull**

There is no Firepower OS Protocol in the game that competes. **Explosive Round is mandatory for this build.**

Alternative supporting protocols (slot in the purple OS effects):
- **Dominating Presence:** Multi-Shot per enemy within 20m
- **Augmented Lethality:** Firepower + Weapon Damage + Incoming Healing per enemy within 20m
- **Pressure Cooker:** Crit Chance when taking damage

## Duality vs Socialite — Chest Choice

Two competing chest talents for this build, both Falen Attack rolls:

### Duality (preferred)
- *"While above 50% HP: +15% Weapon Damage. While below 50% HP: −X% damage taken (DR scaling)."*
- Pure damage above half HP. Below half HP, you get a survivability layer that helps you live to be revived.
- **This is the recommended pick** for full-meta play.

### Socialite 2 (alternative)
- *"When no enemies within X meters: +14% Movement Speed."*
- Kiting / repositioning bonus. Helpful for solo play but does not add raw damage.

**Verdict:** Run **Duality** in groups (you stay above 50% HP behind your Bulwark). Run **Socialite 2** if you are solo and need the mobility to disengage.

### Backpack — Street Fighter (locked-in pick)
- *"+Damage Reduction when enemies are close. +15% Weapon Crit Chance for 5 seconds when an enemy dies within 20m."*
- The crit chance on kill is the engine — every shotgun kill within 20m re-arms a 15% WCC window.
- The DR layer compensates for your Glass Cannon-tier squishiness.

## Combat Rotation

1. **Open with Scanning Pulse.** Tag the pack — Vanguard 3pc Optimal Awareness now grants +Crit Chance for every pulsed target within 70m. The pulse itself does +25% WD / +5% Vulnerable to every pulsed target you shoot.
2. **Drop Commando Smart Cover behind you.** This is your DR layer (40% incoming damage reduction).
3. **Wait for your Bulwark to engage.** Let them draw aggro. This build dies to two yellow rounds otherwise.
4. **Push to ≤15m.** Get to Double Barrel effective range.
5. **Pop Tactical Link.** +20% crit chance, free lock-on, 15-second window of peak DPS.
6. **Fire shotgun in ADS.** Aimed crits proc Explosive Round AOE for 18-22k per detonation. With Falen Multi-Shot, expect 2-3 detonations per trigger pull on crit.
7. **FAMAS swap.** When the pack is broken or you need to disengage, swap to FAMAS to clean up.
8. **Re-pulse.** When the Vanguard pulse cooldown is up, re-pulse the next pack. The Optimal Awareness 3pc is per-pulse, not permanent.

## Why You Need a Bulwark

This is the only major weakness. Quoting Godlike directly:

> *"We are extremely squishy. So we just need for the NPCs to not shoot at us. So having a Bulwark to open the shield and make the NPCs really angry with him is critical."*

The build has:
- ~6,000-8,000 armor (low for Vanguard endgame)
- Glass Cannon levels of damage but **no Glass Cannon talent** — the squishiness is from pure Firepower core stacking with minimal Toughness investment
- Smart Cover DR (40%) helps but does not save you from a focused yellow elite

**Without a Bulwark teammate, you must:**
- Stick to Smart Cover religiously
- Lead with FAMAS until pack is broken
- Use Street Fighter backpack DR carefully
- Solo legendary content is borderline — possible but punishing

The build is designed for **group play**, specifically with a Bulwark shield tank pulling aggro.
