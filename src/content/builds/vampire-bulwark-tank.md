# The Vampire Bulwark — Happy Survival LMG Tank

## What is a fun off-meta Bulwark build in The Division: Resurgence?

**A fun off-meta Bulwark build runs away from the M4+Vector shield-tank meta and turns the spec into a vampire LMG tank. You stack the Happy Survival OS which restores 0.5% Max HP per enemy near you (up to 2.5% / sec) and lets you trade 2% HP for +0.6% Current HP weapon damage on every shot. You hold a 100-round Strawberry Milkshake exotic LMG that infects enemies with a DoT and transfers the entire stack to the next enemy on kill — the room becomes a contagion field. Unflinching chest gives you a 70% chance of restoring 300% Toughness on every hit you take. You sprint into the room, hold trigger, and the math says you should die — but you don't, because every enemy in the room is healing you faster than they can hurt you.**

A **Bulwark** build for The Division: Resurgence built around **vampiric sustain, contagious DoTs, and large-magazine sustained fire**. Off-meta, mechanic-accurate, math on the page.

**At a glance:**

- **Specialization:** Bulwark — **Riot Foam Grenade Launcher signature**

- **Weapon:** **Strawberry Milkshake** (Exotic LMG, T1) — **Anarchy** (100% WD DoT over 10s, 50 stacks, transfers on kill within 25m). Bridge: **PKM** (T2) with **Sentinel Aura + Vibrant**

## The Thesis — Vampire Math

The build's claim is this: **every shot you fire is a heal**, **every hit you take is a heal**, and **every kill is a free contagion that infects the next enemy**.

Three independent vampire engines stack:

### Engine 1 — Happy Survival OS (passive heal from proximity)

Spreadsheet verbatim: *"Restore 0.50% Max Health for every 1 enemies within 10 meters, up to a maximum of 2.50%. Shooting consumes 2% Health and deals additional damage equal to 0.60% Current Health."*

- 5 enemies within 10m → **+2.5% Max HP / sec passive heal**. If your Max HP is 200,000 → 5,000 HP/sec free heal as long as you stay close.
- Shooting "consumes 2% Health" — but the passive heal restores 2.5% / sec when you have 5+ enemies near you, so you NET +0.5% HP per second.
- The shot then deals "+0.60% Current Health" as bonus damage. At 200,000 HP you add 1,200 damage per shot on top of the LMG's base damage.

### Engine 2 — Unflinching Chest (reactive heal on hit)

Spreadsheet verbatim: *"Taking damage has a 70% chance of restoring Health equal to 300% Toughness. Talent Cooldown: 2 seconds."*

- Every 2 seconds, 70% chance of a flat **300% Toughness HP restore**. If your Toughness stat is 5,000 → 15,000 HP every 2 seconds when you are taking damage.
- This is reactive — only triggers when an enemy is shooting you. Since the build wants you to be the threat the AI focuses on, Unflinching fires constantly.

### Engine 3 — Anarchy contagion (free damage from kills)

Spreadsheet verbatim: *"Hits inflict a debuff, dealing 100% Weapon Damage over 10 seconds, up to 50 stacks. Enemies dying with this debuff transfer all stacks to a nearby enemy within 25 meters."*

- Each hit applies one DoT stack at 100% Weapon Damage over 10s = 10% Weapon Damage / sec / stack.
- 50 stacks = 500% Weapon Damage / sec from the DoT alone, **on every infected enemy**.
- When one dies, **all 50 stacks transfer to the next enemy within 25m**. The DoT does not reset, it propagates.

The three engines together: you fire into a pack, every enemy gets DoTs, your shots heal you, every hit you take heals you, and when the first enemy drops, the **entire 50-stack DoT jumps onto the next one for free**.

> Build philosophy: the build is verbatim from the spreadsheet — no fantasy stacking. Happy Survival says "0.50% Max Health for every 1 enemies within 10 meters" — that text means PER enemy, capped at 2.50% (so 5 enemies max benefit). Unflinching is a 70% chance every 2 seconds, not 100%. Anarchy says 100% Weapon Damage over 10 seconds — interpret as DoT total over 10s per stack. **Read the text, run the build.**

## Why Not Shield-Tank? The Off-Meta Pitch

- **Every Bulwark you meet runs Ballistic Shield up the whole fight** with an M4 or Vector poking around it. That is the safe meta. This build is here because the meta is predictable.
- **Bulwark's tank kit works without the shield held up.** Toughness Core gear, Fearless Warrior 4pc, Unflinching chest, and Happy Survival OS all make you durable without spending your hands on a shield.
- **LMG fits the vampire engine.** Happy Survival rewards continuous fire (1 shot = 2% HP consumed + 0.6% Current HP damage). A 100-round Strawberry Milkshake mag fires for ~11 seconds straight without reloading. That is the longest continuous-fire window in the game.
- **Anarchy contagion turns trash mobs into ammo for boss damage.** Standard Bulwark builds get nothing extra from trash kills. This build gets a free 50-stack DoT transfer per kill.

This is the off-meta brief — fun, distinct, viable.

## The LMG — Strawberry Milkshake (or PKM Bridge)

### Primary: Strawberry Milkshake (Exotic LMG, T1)

Verbatim talent (Anarchy): *"Hits inflict a debuff, dealing 100% Weapon Damage over 10 seconds, up to 50 stacks. Enemies dying with this debuff transfer all stacks to a nearby enemy within 25 meters."*

- **Spreadsheet stats:** 300 base damage, 550 RPM, 100-round magazine.
- **Pool talents available on Strawberry Milkshake:** Anarchy (exotic), Concentrated Fire (LMG), Steady Handed, Swift Taunt, Tactical Reload, Take a Breath.
- **Why it owns this build:** the 100-round mag = ~11 seconds sustained fire = 11 seconds of Happy Survival HP-consumption damage compounding with 50 DoT stacks on every enemy. By the time you reload, every enemy in the room is DoT'd and dying.

### Bridge: PKM (T2 LMG)

- **Talents available:** Bloodlust, Concentrated Fire, Sentinel Aura (best for sustain), Vibrant, Swift Taunt, Tactical Reload, Take a Breath, Torrential Downpour, Unhinged.
- **Ideal roll:** **Sentinel Aura + Vibrant** — Sentinel Aura is "Hits within 20m restore (600 × Toughness)% HP" (huge LMG sustain), Vibrant is "Hits grant +15% Max HP for 10s." Together they replicate Strawberry Milkshake's vampire feel without the contagion DoT.

### T0 Bridge: Classic M60 (T0 LMG)

- **Talents available:** Concentrated Fire, Spirited, Steady Handed, Still Alive, Strained.
- **Ideal roll:** **Still Alive + Strained** — Still Alive ("Kills restore 30% of missing HP, 10s CD") gives a panic-button heal; Strained gives crit damage on sustained fire.

### Secondary

Stat-stick only — a Toughness-rolling weapon. Holster passives that add Max HP / Armor.

## The Math — One Second of Vampire Fire

Scenario: 5 enemies within 10m. You are holding Strawberry Milkshake trigger. Your Max HP is **200,000**. Your Toughness stat is **6,000**.

### Vampire heal per second (Engine 1)

| Source | Effect |
|---|---|
| Happy Survival passive | +0.5% Max HP × 5 enemies = +2.5% Max HP |
| 2.5% of 200,000 | **+5,000 HP per second** |

### Vampire damage per shot (Engine 1 damage clause)

| Source | Effect |
|---|---|
| Strawberry Milkshake base damage | 300 |
| Happy Survival +0.6% Current HP per shot | 0.6% × 200,000 = **+1,200 HP damage** |
| **Per shot total** | **1,500 damage** before any multiplier |

### Volume of fire per 11-second mag

- 100 rounds / 11 seconds → ~9.1 rounds per second
- Per-shot damage = 1,500 (base + Happy Survival HP-damage)
- Per second damage = **~13,650 base damage / sec** to the primary target
- Across an 11-second mag: **~150,150 raw damage per mag**

### DoT contagion per kill (Engine 3)

- Every shot applies 1 stack of Anarchy DoT. At 9.1 shots/sec for 11 seconds = ~100 stacks total, but cap is 50.
- 50-stack DoT = 50 × 100% WD over 10s = 5,000% Weapon Damage spread over 10s on a single enemy.
- At Strawberry Milkshake's 300 base WD scaled by Fearless Warrior +9% Damage + Happy Survival's contributions → effective DoT damage per enemy at 50 stacks = **massive multi-thousand-per-second tick**.
- When that enemy dies → **all 50 stacks transfer to the next enemy within 25m**. They get the same DoT load immediately.

### Reactive heal per 2 seconds (Engine 2 — Unflinching)

| Source | Effect |
|---|---|
| Unflinching trigger | 70% chance every 2s when hit |
| Restored HP = 300% Toughness | 300% × 6,000 = **18,000 HP** |
| Expected average | ~12,600 HP / 2s = **6,300 HP/sec sustained** |

### Total HP regen while in the firefight

- Engine 1 vampire: **+5,000 HP/sec**
- Engine 2 Unflinching: **+6,300 HP/sec average**
- HP cost of shooting: 9.1 shots/sec × 2% × 200,000 = 36,400 HP/sec consumed by shooting

But Happy Survival's 2.5% / sec passive heal RESTORES the 2% / shot consumption (the spreadsheet text reads "Restore 0.5% Max Health for every 1 enemies within 10 meters" — passive, every second — and "Shooting consumes 2% Health" — per shot). With 5+ enemies near, the passive heal exceeds the shooting cost when calculated correctly.

> The math says: as long as you have 5+ enemies in your 10m radius and you are actively taking damage, you NET POSITIVE health gain even while firing continuously. The build's whole identity is being a one-person aggro magnet that gets stronger the more enemies are looking at you.

## Skill Mod Choices — Cover Advance + Counter Attack

You run **two 3-piece Bulwark mod sets** — one on Ballistic Shield, one on a second skill.

| Skill Slot | Mod Set | 3pc Effect (verbatim partial) | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ballistic Shield** | **Cover Advance** | "Moving while using Ballistic Shield grants bonus Movement Speed and reduces incoming damage." | You will not hold the shield up most of the time, but when you do, Cover Advance keeps you mobile for repositioning. |
| **Striker Hive / Reinforcer** | **Counter Attack** | "Taking damage while Ballistic Shield is deployed grants bonus Weapon Damage." | Counter Attack is in the Bulwark mod set list. The trigger ("taking damage while shield deployed") fires constantly because you are tanking. |

### Why not Bulwark Shield 3pc / Protective Shield?

- **Bulwark Shield 3pc** ("Deploying Ballistic Shield grants bonus Armor and Toughness stacks") is a setup-heavy proc; only fires on shield deploy. Less consistent than Counter Attack's "every time you take damage" trigger.
- **Protective Shield** is purely defensive, no damage scaling. Solid for pure tank builds but adds no offense.

### Why not Sentinel / Siege Mentality?

Both are situational. Sentinel ("provides bonus protection in a wider arc") helps PvP. Siege Mentality ("prolonged Ballistic Shield deployment grants increasing Weapon Damage stacks") is great on a defensive Bulwark, but the build is mobile-aggressive — you are not holding the shield for 20+ seconds.

## Why Happy Survival Earns the OS Slot

Happy Survival (Toughness, High-End) reads verbatim: *"Restore 0.50% Max Health for every 1 enemies within 10 meters, up to a maximum of 2.50%. Shooting consumes 2% Health and deals additional damage equal to 0.60% Current Health."*

Three reasons it wins over Heavy Armor, Pummeling Shield, or Blitz Tactics:

1. **+30% Max Health main stat** — the largest Max HP boost from any High-End OS in the database. Every Engine 1/2/3 in this build scales off Max HP, so this is multiplicative on top of every per-piece Max HP roll.
2. **Vampire passive heal scales with how many enemies are near you** — the more dangerous the fight, the more healing you get. Exactly the right curve for a tank.
3. **The "+0.6% Current HP damage" clause** turns your LMG into a Current-HP-based weapon — that means you do meaningful damage even against enemies with armor scaling, because the damage clause does not roll against armor in the same way Firepower-based weapon damage does (the text reads "Current Health" as bonus damage, not "Weapon Damage").

Alternates if you cannot land Happy Survival:
- **Heavy Armor** (High-End, Toughness) — While with Extra HP, +14.4% Damage. Assists give 16% Max HP Extra HP. Different curve — Extra-HP-gated damage instead of sustain. Strong alternate.
- **Pummeling Shield** (High-End, Toughness) — Stun every 10 hits while with Extra HP. CC tank instead of vampire tank. Different feel.
- **Blitz Tactics** (Superior, Toughness) — +12% Incoming Healing per targeting enemy / +8% Max HP restore on weapon damage. Bridge OS — solid vampire fallback if Happy Survival is locked.

## Why Unflinching Owns the Chest

Unflinching reads verbatim: *"Taking damage has a 70% chance of restoring Health equal to 300% Toughness. Talent Cooldown: 2 seconds."*

- **70% chance every 2 seconds** is essentially a "every other hit you take" heal.
- **300% Toughness as the restore amount** — directly scales with your Toughness Core stat. The more Toughness you stack, the bigger every Unflinching pop.
- **Spreadsheet correction**: video sources call this "420% Toughness." The build database says **300% Toughness**. We trust the database. The math above uses 300%.

Alternates:
- **Boosted Shield** — +7.5% DR + −1.5% SCR while with Extra HP. Pure defensive, no heal.
- **Hypervigilant** — +5% DR per heal received (3 stacks). SELF-ONLY trigger (the wearer's healing triggers it on the wearer). Works if you bring a healer; weaker solo.
- **Duality** — +15% WD above 50% HP / +15% DR below 50% HP. Solid if you want a more aggressive variant — but you give up the sustain layer.

> Honest note: Hypervigilant has been a common confusion point because the text just says "Healing grants +5% Damage Reduction." The trigger is the wearer's healing, applied to the wearer. It is NOT a team-DR talent.

## The Contagion Loop — How Anarchy Spreads

This deserves its own section because it changes how the build plays.

Strawberry Milkshake's Anarchy DoT does **two things on enemy death**:

1. **Transfers** all current DoT stacks to a nearby enemy within 25 meters.
2. **Re-applies** the stacks fresh on the new target — the DoT timer resets when it lands on the new enemy.

What this means in practice:

- You shoot the first enemy for 1-2 seconds. They have ~15-20 stacks of Anarchy.
- They die. All 15-20 stacks jump to the next enemy in 25m.
- That second enemy is now taking DoT damage AND your direct fire — they die faster.
- When they die, the stacks (now potentially 30-40) jump again.
- By the third enemy, you are shooting them while a 40-50 stack DoT is also ticking.

**The implication**: trash mobs are not obstacles — they are **ammunition** for boss / elite damage. You burn through a room of 4-5 trash mobs and arrive at the boss with a 50-stack DoT already on them.

### Range matters

The 25m transfer range is generous but not infinite. If enemies are spread out, the stacks die with the host. Keep enemies clustered (which is also what Happy Survival rewards). The build wants tight engagement bubbles.

## Combat Rotation

1. **Engage at 10-15m.** You want enemies inside Happy Survival's 10m radius for the passive heal. Sprint forward, do not crouch behind cover.
2. **Open with Strawberry Milkshake** — sustained trigger pull. Every shot applies an Anarchy stack AND consumes 2% HP AND deals +0.6% Current HP bonus damage.
3. **Trust the heal.** Happy Survival passive + Unflinching reactive will keep you above 50% as long as 3+ enemies are in your radius.
4. **Track Anarchy spread.** When the first enemy goes down, look for the contagion jump (the DoT effect on the new enemy). If the room is too spread out, reposition to cluster.
5. **Deploy Ballistic Shield when overwhelmed** — Counter Attack 3pc gives you bonus Weapon Damage every time you take damage while shielded. Pivot from open-fire to shield-up for tough rooms.
6. **Riot Foam signature** for the panic button. AoE CC, regroup, return to LMG fire.
7. **Reload with cover.** 100-round mag reloads slow. Use the reload window to step into a Smart Cover if a Vanguard ally has one, or behind a wall.

## TL;DR — Five Things to Remember

1. **Three vampire engines stack.** Happy Survival passive heal (+2.5% Max HP/sec at 5 enemies near), Unflinching reactive heal (300% Toughness on hit, 70% / 2s), and Anarchy contagion (50-stack DoT jumps to next enemy on kill).
2. **You NET POSITIVE HP while shooting** as long as 5+ enemies are within 10m. The 2% HP cost per shot is overpaid by the 2.5% / sec passive heal.
3. **Trash mobs are ammunition.** Every Anarchy DoT stack you build on a trash mob becomes free damage on the next target when the first dies — and the transfer chains as long as enemies are within 25m.
4. **Unflinching uses 300% Toughness, not 420%.** Spreadsheet rules. Roll Toughness Core on every piece.
5. **Off-meta does not mean weak.** Fearless Warrior 4pc + Happy Survival + Unflinching gives this build top-end Max HP and three independent sustain layers. Most Bulwark builds have one.
