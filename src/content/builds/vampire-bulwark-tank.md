# Bulwark — Happy Survival Vampire Tank

**By RapidF5**

## What it is

Are you tired of taking cover? Do you look at a health bar and think, "I could just shoot my way out of this"? Welcome to the Happy Survival Vampire Tank. 

This is a solo-first, off-meta Bulwark build that turns you into a literal bloodsucking parasite with a machine gun. Using the **Fearless Warrior 4pc**, the **Strawberry Milkshake** exotic LMG, and the **Happy Survival OS**, the premise is beautifully simple: **Hold the trigger until the bad guys stop moving, and watch your HP bar go brrrrrrr.** 

You are a mosquito. A very angry, heavily armored mosquito.

## How it works (aka The Math Behind the Madness)

Three survival engines run while you fire, creating a loop of unkillable annoyance:

1. **Happy Survival OS:** Shooting restores 0.4% Max HP per hit. If you empty a 50-round LMG mag with 100% accuracy, you just gained 20% of your Max HP back. Who needs medkits when you have bullets?
2. **Anarchy (Strawberry Milkshake Exotic Talent):** You deal 100% Weapon Damage as a DoT (Damage over Time) over 10 seconds, stacking up to 50 times. On kill, this glorious hemorrhage transfers to the next enemy within 25m. You are literally applying 50 stacks of bleed across the entire room while your LMG just goes *rattattattatta*. 
3. **Unflinching Chest:** You have a 70% chance to restore HP equal to 300% of your Toughness every time you take a hit. That means when the enemy shoots you, *they are actively healing you*. 

Layered on top of this beautiful nonsense:
- **Fearless Warrior 4pc:** +6% Damage Reduction, +9% Damage Bonus, +18% Toughness. Because why be just a tank when you can be a tank that hits back?
- **Combat Inspiration Backpack:** +18% Weapon Damage to allies within 20m. You're a team player, theoretically.
- **Riot Foam Grenade Launcher:** Lock down the entire pack in sticky white foam for a free DPS window. 

## Why it works

The Strawberry Milkshake's Anarchy talent combined with an LMG maximizes your shots fired per minute. That is the *exact* stat the Happy Survival OS scales with. **Every trigger pull is a heal tick.**

The Toughness cores and Unflinching chest mean that every incoming hit is partially refunded. Your net HP loss in a sustained brawl is basically zero. You are a financial loophole in the game's damage economy. 

Plus, the Anarchy DoT means you don't even need good aim. Just apply 50 stacks to some poor soul, walk away, and watch the entire pack die of mysterious bleeding while you heal yourself with the next mag.

## Rotation (How to be a Nuisance)

1. Push into the pack with your Ballistic Shield up like a very aggressive riot cop.
2. Open with the LMG. Start building those Anarchy stacks. 
3. Happy Survival OS heals you per shot fired. **DO. NOT. LET. GO. OF. THE. TRIGGER.**
4. Watch Unflinching proc on incoming hits (70% chance to gain +300% Toughness as HP). Laugh as they try to kill you.
5. Drop your Reinforcer Hive to cover your back.
6. Use the Riot Foam signature on elites or when you get overwhelmed. Foam them up for a free, non-consensual DPS window. 
7. Kill one enemy and watch the Anarchy DoT spread to the next victim like a highly contagious disease.
