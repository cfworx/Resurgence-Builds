## Overview

This Vanguard build turns you into an immovable frontline anchor. You'll absorb massive damage while your squad operates freely behind you. It's optimized for endgame PvE — strongholds, incursions, and legendary missions.

The core philosophy: **stay in their face, never die, keep their attention.**

## Gear Setup

### Weapons

| Slot | Weapon | Talent |
|------|--------|--------|
| Primary | ACS-12 Shotgun | Preservation |
| Secondary | Police M4 | Flatline |

The ACS-12 with Preservation is your bread and butter. Close-range kills trigger armor repair, synergizing perfectly with the Vanguard's frontline playstyle. The Police M4 serves as a mid-range fallback when you need to pick off targets from cover.

### Gear Set Bonus

Running 4-piece **Foundry Bulwark** gives you:
- **2-piece:** +10% Armor
- **3-piece:** +3% Armor Regeneration per second
- **4-piece:** Shield generates bonus armor over time. Damage absorbed by shield contributes to bonus armor generation.

- Main Stat: Armor +14,200

- Talent: Killing an enemy within 10 meters restores 15% armor

## Playstyle Guide

1. **Open with shield deployed** — walk directly toward enemy groups
2. **Use ACS-12 at close range** — Preservation procs keep you topped off
3. **Pop Chem Launcher at your feet** when armor drops below 50%
4. **Swap to M4** for distant targets or when repositioning
5. **Stay aggressive** — the 4-piece Foundry bonus rewards taking hits
