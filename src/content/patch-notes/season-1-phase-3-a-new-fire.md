Phase 3 of Season 1 is live. The dev team shipped one of the chunkiest update lists Resurgence has seen since launch: a brand new exotic shotgun, a full Classified Ops Pass refresh, an OS Protocol buff that is going to flip the Demolitionist meta, a significant correction to several specialization damage profiles, and a long parade of bug fixes that finally close the door on cards the community has been watching on the [Trello board](https://trello.com/b/qTP1a9cv) for a month and a half.

If you only have time for the headline: the Scorpio exotic shotgun is free in the new Ops Pass, the inventory disappearance bug is officially fixed, Dark Zone spawns now have a ten second immunity window, Applied Demolition almost doubled, and Thermobaric Grenades got brought back to earth. Now the rest.

---

## New Content

### The Scorpio Exotic Shotgun

Pulled directly from the [official Ubisoft announcement](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence/news-updates/wE8oLT7RqyLCfnCJomXij/a-new-fire-season-1-phase-3): "The Scorpio exotic weapon can be grabbed for free in the Classified Ops Pass. This shotgun applies up to 7 stacks of Venom onto your target (lasting 2.5 seconds each) with extra bonuses for each new applied stack. This could be your next ally to take out beefy opponents!"

In plain language: it stacks a damage over time poison, the dot lasts 2.5 seconds per stack, and the gun rewards you for keeping pressure up to seven stacks deep. We were tracking the Scorpio in our [Scorpio and Diamondback leak post](/news/scorpio-diamondback-coming-to-division-resurgence-june-2026/) earlier this season and it has now landed officially. Time to update your loadouts.

If you want to give your Scorpio some flair, the **Lethal Stinger** exotic weapon skin is also in the Ops Pass.

### New Classified Ops Pass

50 tier track. Free Scorpio at the headline. The new **Freemen Apparel Set** is the full cosmetic chase this phase.

### Store Cosmetics

From the official announcement: Boombox and King's Crown Jewel weapon skins, plus three apparel sets running on a limited window: 4th of July, Tactical Diver, and Cleaner.

## Gameplay and Combat

These are the changes that will actually move builds. Quoted from the dev patch notes shared on the [official Reddit post](https://www.reddit.com/r/thedivisionresurgence/comments/1uclqwl/getting_ready_for_season_1_phase_3/).

### Cover and Cutscene Fix

Fixed an issue where players could be forced out of cover when a cutscene started. If you have ever popped out of cover into a sniper round because a cinematic triggered, that door is now closed.

### Focused Defense Talent

The cooldown reduction on Focused Defense has been corrected. The talent was not behaving as documented; it should now reduce ability cooldown as stated.

### Combat Medic Courage Task Requirement

The Courage task that asked for 3,000,000 units now asks for 5,000. Yes, you read that right. The dev team noted: players who already had 5,000 Courage units banked will need to earn fresh Courage units after the patch to actually complete the task. The original target was clearly a typo. The fix is here.

### Dark Zone Spawn Immunity

Spawn and respawn in the Dark Zone now grant ten seconds of immunity. Firing your weapon, using a skill, or throwing a grenade cancels the immunity early. Spawn camping in the Dark Zone just got demoted from a strategy to a hobby.

### Applied Demolition OS Protocol Buff

Skill critical damage moved from **24 / 28.8 / 33.6 / 38.4 / 43.2 / 48 percent** to **43.2 / 51.84 / 60.48 / 69.12 / 77.76 / 86.2 percent** across the six ranks. The Trello card flagged this for a future balance pass. That balance pass is this one. Demolitionists, your Applied Demolition is roughly doubled at top rank. The [Jackpot Striker drone build](/builds/jackpot-striker-drone-tech-operator-build/) just got significantly more interesting.

### Thermobaric Grenades Nerf

| Stat | Old Values | New Values |
|---|---|---|
| Damage | 460 / 540 / 620 / 700% | 300 / 350 / 400 / 450% |
| Radius Increase | 100% | 30% |

The Trello board flagged Thermobaric Barrel as potentially overperforming earlier this month. This is the correction. The damage cut is substantial and the radius got brought way down. If your build was carried mostly by Thermobaric output, it is time to pivot toward Applied Demolition crit damage scaling.

### Armor Strengthening Mod Set

Fixed an issue where the Armor Strengthening combo set was not detecting proximity to the chemical trap. The Trello card had this marked as fix ready since June 9. It is now live. The [Muscular Nurse build](/builds/muscular-nurse-combat-medicine-build-nadie/) leans on Armor Strengthening combo set behavior, so retest it in your usual content.

### Specialization Damage Corrections

Multiple specializations were doing more damage than intended on a regular interval. The dev team called out:

- **Tech Operator Aegis and Combat Medic** were dealing more damage than designed at regular intervals. The community has been calling these "Aegis Burst" and "Medic Hypershot" respectively, and both are now corrected.
- **Juggernaut and Recon** were passively dealing more damage than intended. Both have been brought back in line.

The dev team explicitly noted that they will be monitoring the impact of these corrections to make sure these specializations remain effective in the actual content. They are watching the data, and adjustment patches are on the table if anything tips too far down.

## Progression and Inventory

### Items Disappearing From Inventory Is Fixed

The card the entire subreddit has been waiting on. The dev notes confirm: an issue causing some items to disappear from inventory has been resolved. If you have been screenshotting your stash every week out of paranoia, you can probably stop. Probably.

### Weapon Preview and Display Errors

Fixed preview errors and incorrect visualizations on weapons in menus.

### Aim and Grenade Interaction Fixes

Fixed interactions that could interrupt aiming or grenade use.

### Elite Mission Crate Fix

Fixed an issue where players could not open the high-end equipment and weapon crates received after completing the Elite mission.

### Strawberry Milkshake Cleanup

Three Strawberry Milkshake specific fixes:

1. The exotic was incorrectly showing damage as "Piercing" instead of "Crushing" in the Armory. Fixed.
2. The weapon was dropping with Level 1 talents instead of Level 2 talents like every other exotic. Fixed.
3. **Reissue:** the dev team is sending an additional upgraded Strawberry Milkshake to every player who had unlocked it before this patch (one per copy you owned). Check your in-game mail.

## Events and Activities

### Outpost Annihilation

The card has been on Trello since May 19. The dev notes for this patch confirm: the Outpost Annihilation completion issue, where objectives would fail to advance after defeating the Rikers, is resolved.

### Event Timers and Display

Event timers and the display inconsistencies that showed up when players were offline have been fixed. Event UI and reward display have also been improved.

### Speedrun Challenge Squad Leaderboard

Players can no longer receive rewards multiple times from the Speedrun Challenge squad leaderboard by playing with different squads. The exploit window has closed.

## Social and Online

- **General Chat is now gated by progression.** Characters unlock General Chat once they reach the open world New York Base of Operations. The team called this out as a measure to reduce spam.
- Fixed an issue where guest account progression could be lost during account linking. Anyone who has been playing on a guest account, this fix protects your progress when you finally link to Ubisoft Connect.

## Store Changes

With Phase 3, the dev team also rolled out store changes effective June 24.

### Store Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where purchasing items in the store with real money could trigger network errors
- Fixed several purchase issues affecting Battle Pass and store transactions
- Fixed discounted price display
- Fixed overlapping UI elements in store screens
- Fixed missing or incorrectly available items in the store

### Sealed Crate Drop Rate Improvements

The drop rate buff is significant across all three Sealed Crate categories.

**Standard Sealed Crate**

| Stat | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Tier S drop rate | 0.5% | 2.0% |
| Tier A drop rate | 7.5% | 10% |
| Tier S guaranteed pity | 90 pulls | 50 pulls |

Standard Sealed Crate content adjustments:
- **Tier S:** added Superior Weapon Parts x3 and Superior Protective Plating x3
- **Tier B:** added Standard Weapon Parts x5 and Standard Protective Plating x5; removed Worn Metal Components x5 and Worn Fabrics x5

**OS Protocol Sealed Crate**

| Stat | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Tier S drop rate | 0.5% | 2.0% |
| Tier A drop rate | 7.5% | 10% |
| Tier S guaranteed pity | 90 pulls | 50 pulls |

**Specialization Sealed Crate**

| Stat | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Tier S drop rate | 0.5% | 2.0% |
| Tier A drop rate | 7.5% | 10% |
| Tier S guaranteed pity | 90 pulls | 50 pulls |

A four times Tier S rate across the board, with the guaranteed pity timer cut nearly in half. For agents who have been opening crates with the resigned face of a man checking the weather forecast for his daughter's outdoor wedding, this is a meaningful change.

## What This Means for Your Builds

A few quick reads on how this patch moves the meta:

- **Demolitionists got a massive trade.** Applied Demolition went up, Thermobaric Grenades came down. If your build was carried mostly by Thermobaric output, swap toward Applied Demolition crit damage scaling.
- **Aegis Tech Operator builds need a retest.** The Aegis Burst correction is officially shipped. Your damage profile in elite content will look different starting today. See the [Aegis Tech Operator build](/builds/aegis-chad-glass-cannon-tech-operator-build-ifozzy/) for the baseline.
- **Bulwark Juggernaut and Recon builds are slightly tamer.** The passive damage adjustment hits both. The [Involuntary Nap Time Bulwark Juggernaut build](/builds/involuntary-nap-time-bulwark-juggernaut-build/) is still strong because the build's identity is stun control and tankiness, not raw damage scaling.
- **Combat Medic builds should reassess.** Medic Hypershot and Armor Strengthening proximity behavior both changed. The [Muscular Nurse Combat Medicine build](/builds/muscular-nurse-combat-medicine-build-nadie/) leans on Armor Strengthening, so retest it in your usual content.
- **Scorpio is the new toy.** Free in the Ops Pass, stacks venom, scales with stack count. We are working on a dedicated build write-up.
