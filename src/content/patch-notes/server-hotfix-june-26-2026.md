Heads up, agents. The dev team is rolling out a server-side hotfix right now. You may be kicked back to the title screen and need to reconnect over the next few hours. This is the first follow-up patch since Season 1 Phase 3 "A New Fire" went live on June 24, and it lands on a few of the biggest community pain points from the last 48 hours: the iOS crash spike, server latency, the General Chat unlock requirement getting tuned, and a damage reduction cap that will quietly change how a lot of tanky builds feel in PvP.

---

## What Changed (Verbatim from the Dev Tweet)

The patch notes posted by [@TheDivMobile](https://x.com/TheDivMobile/status/2070483985642197252):

### Fixes

- Fixed a bug that caused the game to crash on iOS.
- Fixed an issue that was causing server latency issues.
- Reactivated the General in-game chat, now only available to characters having reached the open-world (or the NYC base of operations).
- Fixed an issue where the DPAD-UP button on controller could, in some situations, open a menu instead of triggering gameplay actions.

### Lone Wolf Challenge

- Lone Wolf Challenge timer trigger is updated to start if an enemy takes damage (even if the doors are not opened).

### Damage Reduction Balancing

- Damage reduction has been capped at a maximum of 80%. Please note that this is a server-based change with player-facing visual feedback to be added at a later date.

### Pummeling Shield Balancing

- Added a 10 second cooldown to the stun effect.
- Reduced the stun duration from 2 seconds to 1.5 seconds.

---

## What This Actually Means for You

### iOS Players Get Their Game Back

If you have been getting kicked out at the mission loading screen since Phase 3 dropped, the iOS crash fix is the one you have been waiting on. Update through the App Store and try again.

### General Chat Is Back

Phase 3 had General Chat completely off while the new gating system rolled out. It is now reactivated, with the new rule: your character has to reach the open world (the NYC Base of Operations) before General Chat unlocks. Brand new agents still cannot use it from the tutorial. Anyone past the opening should see chat working again after reconnecting.

### Controller Players, Read This Twice

The DPAD-UP misfire that opened a menu instead of triggering your bound action is fixed. Anyone running a DualSense or Xbox controller on PC via Ubisoft Connect should immediately notice the difference. Especially nice for [Steam Deck players](/news/division-resurgence-coming-to-steam-pc-2026/) running through Steam Input.

### Lone Wolf Challenge Cheese Closed

You can no longer game the Lone Wolf Challenge timer by stalling outside the door. The clock now starts the moment an enemy takes damage, doors open or not. Speedrunners, recalibrate your routes.

### The Big PvP Change: 80 Percent Damage Reduction Cap

This is the headline. The team has placed a hard ceiling at **80 percent damage reduction**, regardless of stacking. The team called out that this is a server side change and the player-facing visual feedback (your stats screen will eventually reflect it) is coming later. So your sheet might still display higher than 80, but the server is enforcing 80.

For anyone who has been building tank specializations to the absolute limit, this cap removes the stacked invulnerability ceiling that some builds were hitting. Your tankiness still matters; the upper bound is just clipped.

### Pummeling Shield Stun Got the Rolling Pin

**10 second internal cooldown added to the stun effect.** **Stun duration cut from 2 seconds to 1.5 seconds.** Together this is a significant correction to the Pummeling Shield meta. Bulwark Juggernaut builds that were chaining stun lock are losing the chain. Our [Involuntary Nap Time Bulwark Juggernaut build](/builds/involuntary-nap-time-bulwark-juggernaut-build/) is built around stun control and survival; the build still has identity, but the rotation needs a refresh. Builders, update accordingly.

## Connection Loss Window

Per the dev team, expect to reconnect once or twice over the next few hours as the servers cycle. If you get booted mid mission, your loot from that run may not be guaranteed. Wait for a stable connection before opening any high value crates or running anything that costs an entry token.
