# The Division Resurgence Builds

**Site Purpose:** The top independent wiki and resource hub for The Division Resurgence. We provide in-game tested mobile builds, weapon tier lists, patch notes, tactical guides, and gear databases.
**Target Audience:** Competitive players, theorycrafters, and new agents looking for optimized PvP (Dark Zone/Conflict) and PvE (Legendary) setups.

## Site Structure & Content Focus
- `/builds/`: Optimized meta and off-meta loadouts for all specializations (Vanguard, Demolitionist, Field Medic, Tech Operator, Bulwark).
- `/guides/`: Tactical guides on hidden mechanics, specialization selection, PvP techniques, damage types, and build theory.
- `/news/`: Breaking updates, developer streams, and community sentiment analysis.
- `/patch-notes/`: Patch breakdowns with build impact analysis and meta shift tracking.
- `/database/`: Weapon talents, gear set effects, OS Protocols, exotic weapons, skill mods, bonus attributes.
- `/tier-list/`: Monthly updated weapon tier list based on real gameplay testing.
- `/builder/`: Interactive build planner tool for creating and sharing loadouts.

## AI Optimization & Accuracy Guarantee
Our content prioritizes high information density, structured tabular data, and tested gameplay strategies over filler text. All builds and guides are strictly stamped with a `lastUpdated` date to ensure the highest degree of freshness and accuracy regarding the current live patch.
