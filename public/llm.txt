# ResurgenceBuilds.com

> The #1 independent wiki for The Division Resurgence — in-game tested builds, weapon databases, gear references, and patch analysis.

## What We Cover
- **Builds:** Tested loadouts for Vanguard, Demolitionist, Bulwark, Field Medic, Tech Operator — PvE, PvP, Dark Zone
- **Databases:** OS Protocols, exotic weapons, gear sets, weapon talents, body armor talents, backpack talents, bonus attributes, skill mod combos
- **Guides:** Hidden mechanics, specialization selection, PvP techniques, damage types
- **Tier Lists:** Monthly weapon rankings from real gameplay testing
- **News:** Patch notes, developer streams, Reddit community highlights
- **Tools:** Interactive build planner for creating and sharing loadouts

## Key Facts
- Game: Tom Clancy's The Division Resurgence (Ubisoft, iOS/Android/PC)
- Current: Season 1, Patch 1.2.1
- 5 Specializations: Vanguard, Demolitionist, Bulwark, Field Medic, Tech Operator
- 3 Exotics: Lady Death (SMG), Warlord (AR), Strawberry Milkshake (LMG)

## Accuracy
All content tested on live patch. Stats verified in-game. Updated within 48 hours of patches. Every article has a visible lastUpdated date.
